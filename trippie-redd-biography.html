Trippie Redd Bio (Wiki)

Who is Trippie Redd?

Trippie Redd is a popular American rapper.

He is famous for his popular singles like Love Scars, Dark Knight Dummo, and Poles1469.

He works with artists, Lil Yachty, XXXTentacion, Kris Wu, Rich Brian, Joji, and DRAM.

Trippie Redd: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity

His name is Michael Lamar White IV. He is professionally known as Trippie Redd. He was born June 18, 1999, in Canton, Ohio. His father was in prison at the time of his birth. He is raised by his mother.

The rapper has a brother in his family who was a hip-hop performer. He recently died in a car accident in 2014.

Education, School/College University

Redd was good in his studies and obtains a GPA score of 4.0 in high school in Canton, Ohio.

Trippie Redd: Early Life

As Trippie Redd was grown up with his mother. His mother uses to amuse him by playing different music. He uses to listen to Ashanti, Marilyn Manson, Tupac, Nas, and T- Pain.

His music career grew after meeting with Lil Wop. Wop notices that Trippie has the potential to grow his career in rapping. And he helps him to record his first creation named ‘Awakening My Inner Beast’.

Michael has a good beginning in his career and shifted to Los Angeles for starting a serious career as a show business personality.

Trippie Redd: Profession life, Career

Trippie Redd starts his profession as a rapper and achieves great success at a young age. Lil Wop has helped him a lot to grow up professionally.

They collaborated with Kodie Shane and recorded three projects titled Awakening My Inner Beast, Beast Mode, and Rock the World Trippie.

White then eventually get the opportunity to sign to the label Strange Entertainment (now known as Elliot Grainge Entertainment) and relocated to Los Angeles.

In May 2017, White released the debut mixtape, A Love Letter to You. The song received 8 million views on YouTube and 13 million on SoundCloud.

White’s album was in XXXTentacion’s album 17. His song “Fuck Love” was number 41 on the Billboard Hot 100.

In October 2017, White was back with his second mixtape, A Love Letter to You II. The album was able to gain much success by debuting at #34 on the Billboard 200. He collaborates with EP, Atlanta rap artist Lil Wop titled Angels & Demons.

White’s song “Dark Knight Dummo” releases on Dec 5, 2017. The song features Travis Scott. Almost many of the songs are hit. This song peaked at 72 on the Billboard Hot 100.

By this song, White makes out his first entry on the chart as a lead artist. On December 25, 2017, White released the song “TR666” on his SoundCloud account.

It features Swae Lee produced by Scott Storch. The track preview is on November 30. White release “18” alongside Baauer, Kris Wu, Joji, and Rich Brian.

White is seen in an interview with Billboard in March 2018. There he says his debut studio album features collaborations with Lil Wayne and Erykah Badu.

In July 2018, White announced his debut album would be titled Life’s a Trip and be 26 tracks long.

Trippie Redd has outstanding performance and appreciates by everyone. He doesn’t receive any of the awards yet.
