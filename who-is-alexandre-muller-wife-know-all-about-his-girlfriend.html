Who is Alexandre Muller' wife? Know all about his girlfriend

Alexandre Muller is a French tennis player born on 1 February 1997 and his highest ranking to this date by the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) is World No. 96 in singles. He achieved this feat on 10 April, 2023 and he also rose to a career-high ATP doubles ranking of World No. 273 which he achieved on 9 May, 2022.

After turning pro, he mostly played at the ITF Men’s World Tennis Tour and ATP Challenger Tour winning 15 total singles and doubles titles across both competitions. He made his debut at the ATP 150-mark way back in July 2022, which was only a few weeks after winning the Blois Challenger which was also his first Challenger title. The very next season, he made it to his first major final at the ATP Tour at Marrakesh which in order also earned him his top 100 debut.

He made his first Grand Slam debut at the 2017 French Open after receiving a wildcard entry at the singles but was beaten by Thiago Monteiro right in the first round. He also made it to the first round at the 2019 French Open losing this time to Roberto Carballes Baena right at the first round. He made it to his first ATP quarterfinal at the 2023 Qatar ExxonMobil Open after defeating Nikoloz Basilashvili and Botic van de Zandschulp but eventually lost to Andy Murray in the last eight.

Who is Alexandre Muller’s girlfriend?

Alexandre Muller is currently focused only on tennis and has no girlfriends what so ever. His marital status is single and he also doesn’t have any children currently.

Alexandre Muller family

Alexandre Muller who is nicknamed Lamull started playing tennis on the tennis court at his childhood home with his dad at the age of 6. His father, Stefan currently works for BNP Paribas and his mother, Virginie is a mortgage recovery agent and he also has a brother named, Julien.
