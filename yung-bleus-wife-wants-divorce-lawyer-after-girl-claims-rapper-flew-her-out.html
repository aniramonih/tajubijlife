Yung Bleu's wife wants 'divorce lawyer' after girl claims rapper 'flew her out'

Yung Bleu’s wife has called him out on Instagram after a girl claimed he slid into her DMs and flew her to New York on a private jet.

Tiemeria owns her own beauty company Dark Moon Cosmetics and the pair, who have been married for more than a year, share three children together.

Woman claims Yung Bleu ‘flew her out’

The allegations were made by a woman called Tenom who shared a TikTok video outing the celebrity who “flew her out”.

She claimed the rapper slid into her DMs and began talking to her after he saw a video of her long tongue which went viral.

The girl then showed a screenshot of a message conversation with Yung Bleu’s name at the top that appears to show him replying to a picture of her with the word: “Hard.”

“U gone follow back? Lol,” the next message says, and she then replied: “Duhhh it’s you of course. I love your music.”

“He told me that he was doing a soundtrack for Power in New York and he wanted me to come with him to New York,” Tenom continued.

The woman claimed they continued talking and he then paid for her to have her hair done and flew her on a private jet to New York.

She slammed the way he treated her after flying her out, saying he didn’t speak to her or give her any food and sat on his phone while they were out for dinner.

Rapper’s wife calls him out

Just hours after the video was posted, Tiemeria responded on her Instagram story, calling her husband out and saying the girl is “not lying”.

She called him a “sissy b***h” and said she can’t believe he has the “nerve to put somebody on that 1800s a** plane wings”.

“I’m finna embarrass you b***h,” Tiemeria continued before sharing an audio clip of herself claiming the rapper had tried to deny it.

She said Bleu was lying next to her in bed when she confronted him about Tenom’s claims and he “got up and ran to the guest house”.

“He really trying to act like the girl is lying on him… I’m looking at the video this girl posted receipts.”

‘I need the best divorce lawyer’

Following the audio rant, the rapper’s girlfriend claimed she wants a divorce from her husband, the father of her three children.

“I need the best divorce lawyer in Georgia,” she wrote on a black screen. Yung Bleu hasn’t publicly responded to the allegations.

HITC and GRV Media have reached out to the rapper’s representative for comment and have not yet received a response.

Fans are reacting to the drama on Twitter where one wrote “Yung Bleu’s wife went in on him” and another added: “I am dying at Yung Bleu’s wife’s posts.”
