Zach Muckleroy Car Accident: Man Killed in Accident on US Highway 281

Zack Muckelroy, CEO of Muckleroy & Falls, died in a fatal crash on US Highway 281.

Zach Muckleroy, the Chief Executive Officer of Muckleroy & Falls, died in a fatal crash along with two children, Judson and Lindsay, on US Highway 281 in Blanco County. His death was announced on Muckleroy & Falls Instagram account by the company. The post read as,

“It is with heavy hearts that we share the tragic passing of our beloved CEO, Zach Muckleroy, and his two children, Judson and Lindsay.”

Who was Zach Muckleroy?

Zach Muckleroy is Chief Executive Officer of Muckleroy & Falls. He was known for his generosity and kindness. He would light up every room he walked into with his charisma and positivity. He was a longtime resident of Fort Worth, Texas. He was a proud alumnus of TCU.

Construction and building were his passion, and he was an integral part of the construction community through his dedication and hard work. He led the Muckleroy & Falls to progress and poured his heart and soul into providing company’s success. He was known to lead the company with a ‘calming demeanor’ despite the challenging and chaotic nature of the work.

He was a devoted son, a loving husband, and a caring father. He loved his family. His family was his whole world. He would treat everyone with kindness and respect regardless of their position or station. His profound integrity, sensibility, and professionalism earned him respect and admiration among his peers. All those who had the privilege to know him will intensely feel his absence.

What happened to Zach Muckleroy?

The details of his death have not been made public. According to reports, he was involved in an accident on US Highway 281 in Blanco County. He passed away along with his beloved children, Judson and Lindsay. The Texas Department of Public Safety and Blanco County Sheriff’s Office are investigating the incident to learn about the circumstances which led to the devastating event.

The Muckleroy family has suffered an inconsolable loss as three of their beloved family members lost their lives in the tragic accident. Their sudden, unexpected deaths have left the family devastated and the community reeling with shock and grief. The community has offered overwhelming support for the family, expressing condolences and showering the family with kind prayers.

Survived by

He is survived by his wife, Lauren, parents, Harold and Pat; siblings Alyson and Steve Gasper, and David and Madison Muckleroy.

We offer our sincerest condolences to the family and friends. May they find solace in the community’s support during this extremely challenging time.
