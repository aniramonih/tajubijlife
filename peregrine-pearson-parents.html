Peregrine Pearson mother is Marina Rose Cordle, daughter of Conservative politician John Cordle, and Michael Orlando Weetman Pearson, also known as the 4th Viscount Cowdray.

Michael wants to use his fortune to launch several different companies.

Michael and Marina divorced this summer following 36 years of marriage.
