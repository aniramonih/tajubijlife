Olivia Wilde &amp; Pedro Pascal Joined Florence Pugh, Paul Mescal, &amp; More Stars at CAAs Pre-Oscars Pa

CAA is kicking off Oscars weekend with a star-studded-party!

The talent agency hosted their annual Pre-Oscars Party on Friday night (March 10) held at the Sunset Towel Hotel in Los Angeles.

Stars in attended included Olivia Wilde, Pedro Pascal, Zoe Kravitz, Cate Blanchett, Allison Janney, Florence Pugh, Andrew Garfield, Halle Bailey, Rita Ora, Taika Waititi, Miles Teller and wife Keleigh Sperry, Chloe Grace Moretz, KiKi Layne, Elton John and husband David Furnish, Sandra Bullock, Brian Tyree Henry, Demi Moore, Antonio Banderas and girlfriend Nicole Kimpel, Morgan Freeman, tennis star Maria Sharapova, Wednesday‘s Percy Hynes White, Michelle Monaghan, Quinta Brunson, Amy Adams, Vin Diesel, Hailee Steinfeld, James Corden, Richard Madden, Trevor Noah, Lily James, Paul Mescal, Ava DuVernay, Phil Dunster, Jon Hamm and fiancee Anna Osceola, Jonathan Scott, Zooey Deschanel, BJ Novak, Danny DeVito, Simone Ashley, HAIM, Ana de Armas, DeWanda Wise, Eiza Gonzalez, and Ariana DeBose.

The 2023 Oscars are taking place this Sunday (March 12) with host Jimmy Kimmel on ABC starting at 8pm ET. You can check out the full list of nominees here!
