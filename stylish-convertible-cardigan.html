If you’re ready for sweater weather and are looking for easy outerwear that’s both comfortable and street style-worthy, then consider the search over. Your Shop With Us pals have found a convertible cardigan that can be worn in two effortlessly cool ways.

The Gibson Convertible Cozy Fleece Wrap Cardigan is a high-low design made with soft, stretchy fabric. In the front, it gives off the look of a duster while the back sits up near the waist.

If you prefer to bundle up more, the cascading sides can be pulled up and snapped in place for a long-sleeve wrap top style. Check out how it looks after the quick transformation:

If this cozy number had you at “convertible,” then all that is left to do is select your favorite color. There are six options, including neutral shades like the grey above and this pretty soft pink version:

Pair with a T-shirt and jeans for a traditional look. Not a fan of the classics? Wear the garment in its wrap top form with trendy moto-inspired leggings and over-the-knee boots. You can even put a fun fall twist on your summer maxidresses by pairing them with the sweater in its duster form.

Regular sizes range from XS to XL. Petite sizes start at XXS and go to XL. Shoppers who purchased the cardigan noted that it is a little long and recommend ordering one size down.
