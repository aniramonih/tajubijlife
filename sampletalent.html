Hire Ali Siddiq for a Corporate Event or Performance Booking.

Ali Siddiq is a comic speaker. Siddiq, who is from Houston, has been on the comedy circuit for more than 15 years, engaging audiences of various racial, economic, and cultural backgrounds as a headliner in stand-up comedy and in a variety of other media as a writer, producer, and host.

Siddiq began honing his craft at Just Joking in Houston, where he relentlessly pursued the mike before becoming a weekly featured comic. He developed his brand on Just Joking, honed his comedic storytelling abilities, and attracted the attention of some of the best comics in the business. Siddiq's pursuit of this interest paid off as he later went on to tour or collaborate with a number of notable performers, including D.L. Hughley, Bill Bellamy, Bill Burr, Cedric the Entertainer, Angela Johnson, Bobby Lee, Katt Williams, Eddie Griffin, Maz Jobrani, J. Anthony Brown, Bruce Bruce, Mike Epps, Rickey Smiley, and Kevin Hart.

Siddiq performed on four seasons of BET's Comic View, finished in the top three of NBC Universal's Stand Up for Diversity, made an appearance in the season finale of HBO's Def Comedy Jam, and performed on BET's One Mic Stand and Bill Bellamy's Who's Got Jokes, which aired on TV One. Siddiq has contributed his voice and opinion to TV One's Washington Watch, hosted by Roland Martin, in addition to his stand-up performances. This appearance demonstrated his dedication to making sure his audience not only laughed, but also learned something.

Siddiq, who is well known for his ability to engage audiences, also uses this talent to engage with his community. The Jive Turkey Comedy Jam, which is produced by Siddiq, recently gathered canned items for the North Texas Food Bank. He also served as the keynote speaker at the 2012 Freshman Orientation at Texas Southern University. Additionally, Siddiq gave a set titled "Vote for your Dayum Self" at a Texas League of Young Voters Education Fund event that encouraged attendees to vote in the 2012 election..
