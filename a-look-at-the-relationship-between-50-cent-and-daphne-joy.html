A Look at the Relationship Between 50 Cent and Daphne Joy

An American rapper named Diddy was spotted with Daphne Joy earlier this month, and like his previous relationships, this one raised doubts in the eyes of his fans. Joy and Diddy both posted images from the iHeartRadio event and the same flight. Rapper 50 Cent, Joy’s ex, appeared to confirm the rumor when he uploaded a picture of their son Sire, age 10, and himself wearing gold sunglasses, saying sarcastically:

“Oh, that’s your mother over there with Puffy. LOL. You may recall that I recently stated that these b***hes are nuts. SMH.”

In her lengthy response, Daphne Joy said she “hates speaking about her private life on social media” and that the issue needs to be resolved because “she is weary of her story of being what it is.”

After her split from 50 Cent, she allegedly revealed that she is now more focused on her child:

I’ve quietly developed and recovered. I love and appreciate everyone in my life, but when I finally show it, I feel attacked.

Model, actress, presenter, and businesswoman Daphne Joy is well-known. Her father was born in Puerto Rico, while her mother was born in the Philippines, hence she has Filipino and Puerto Rican ancestry.

She started modeling swimsuits for OMG Miami Swimwear and has since introduced the “Daphne Joy for Abyss” Christmas collection by Abby Collection.

At the age of 17, Daphne Joy began her modeling career. Since then, she has walked the runway for well-known fashion houses and appeared in advertisements. She has also participated in the yearly Celebrity Catwalk.

The covers of King Magazine, Ed Hardy Swim Campaign, Show Magazine, Smooth Magazine, BlackMen Magazine, and BlackMen featured Joy. She appeared on CSI: Las Vegas, Criminal Minds, and Curb Your Enthusiasm after making her television debut on MTV in 2007 with Nick Cannon Presents Wild ‘N Out.

50 Cent and Daphne Joy have made minor roles in movies like Venice Heat, Frankenfood, and Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides, as well as in several music videos, such as What You Got, Miss Independent, and others. Joy holds the values and culture of her native Philippines with the highest regard. She supposedly loves to travel, and cook, yet she’s afraid of heights.

Relationship between 50 Cent and Daphne Joy

Although Joy has always made an effort to keep her private life quiet, her relationship with American rapper 50 Cent, who was also born Curtis James Jackson III, helped her gain media attention.

Sire, the couple’s son, was born in 2013, however, tensions between the two rapidly arose after Joy accused Curtis of assaulting her in June 2013 by breaking into her Toluca Lake condominium. According to Joy, Curtis allegedly caused $7,100 worth of property damage.

Curtis was promptly detained and accused of mischief and domestic abuse. However, after he entered a not-guilty plea to petty mischief, the charges were dropped. Joy had previously been linked to reality star Rob Kardashian for a brief while in 2013, and during 2015 and 2016, it appears that she dated musician Jason Derulo.
