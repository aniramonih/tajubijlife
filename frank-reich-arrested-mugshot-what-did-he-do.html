Frank Reich controversy and scandal 

Throughout his career, Frank Reich has been involved in a few controversies and scandals.

In 2018, Reich was chastised for deciding to go for it on fourth and 4 from his 43-yard line with 3 minutes left in the game and the Colts trailing the Houston Texans by 3 points.

The Texans won the game with a field goal on the next drive. Reich defended his decision, saying he would “go for it 10 times out of 10” in that situation.

Reich’s play-calling has also been criticized, particularly during his time with the Carolina Panthers.

The Panthers’ offense finished 30th in total offense and 30th in passing yards per game in 2023.

Despite these issues, Reich is well-liked in the NFL. He is well-known for his offensive versatility and ability to groom young quarterbacks. Reich is a strong leader who has a positive influence on his team.

Following a 1-10 start to the 2023 season, head coach Frank Reich of the Carolina Panthers was fired.

For the remainder of the 2023 season, Chris Tabor will serve as the Panthers’ acting head coach. Tabor is the defensive coordinator and senior assistant head coach for the Panthers.

The Panthers were having a challenging season at the time of Reich’s firing. With one of the worst offenses in the NFL, the team has dropped ten straight games.
