Meet Alison Dickey, John C. Reilly's Wife

Alison Dickey is an independent film producer renowned for her role as an additional crew in the 1988 movie, “Colors.” However, she is most famous as the wife of American actor, comedian, and producer John C. Reilly. Reilly debuted in the Brian De Palma war film “Casualties of War” as PFC Herbert Hatcher in 1989 and has since made a name for himself as an established actor.

Despite being a film producer since the late 80s, Alison Dickey has kept most details about herself away from the media. Nonetheless, fans are curious about the celebrity spouse. Here’s everything we know about John C. Reilly’s wife.

Alison Dickey’s Background

Despite being a celebrity spouse, Alison Dickey has kept details about her upbringing private. Due to her secluded lifestyle, her birth date and exact birthplace are unknown. Along with her birthdate, there are no details regarding the identity of her parents and siblings, if any. In addition, there is no information about her educational background and achievements. 

However, her husband, John C. Reilly, was born the fifth of six children on May 24, 1965, in Chicago, Illinois, U.S. His father was of Irish and Scottish descent, while his mother was of Lithuanian ancestry. Although his parents’ identity is unknown, Reilly’s father ran an Industrial linen supply company. 

Having grown up in a Chicago Lawn neighborhood, Reilly was mischievous during childhood. However, he was raised Roman Catholic and attended Brother Rice High School. Upon graduation from high school, the famous actor enrolled at The Theatre School at Depaul University in Chicago, where he graduated several years later.  

Where Did Alison Dickey Meet John C. Riley?

The famous film producer was an actress in her heyday. She was an additional crew member in several movies. On the set of one of these movies, she met the man who would later become her husband.

Dickey met Reilly in 1989 while filming “Casualties of War in Thailand.” While Reilly was acting, she was part of the additional film crew. The couple hit it off immediately on the set and soon started dating.

Although the exact date is unknown, the pair tied the knot three years after they met in 1992. Since their intimate-style wedding, the couple has kept their marriage out of the spotlight. Despite being famous faces in Hollywood, there is little information about their marriage. However, they seem happy in their little world and appear to be going strong.

Alison Dickey’s Children

Dickey and her husband have been together for over three decades and have welcomed two children. Although the couple tied the knot in the early 90s, they didn’t welcome their first child until about six years later.

The love birds began their parenthood journey after the arrival of their first child and son, Leo Reilly, on September 3, 1998, in California, U.S. Like his mom, there are no details about his early life and educational achievements. However, he has a career in entertainment like his parents.

The couple welcomed their second son, Arlo Reilly, in September 2001. Like his mom and elder brother, he shies away from the public. Most details about his upbringing and educational background are unknown. However, reports suggest he also has an entertainment career like his parents and elder brother.

Although he became famous after a tweet about his looks went viral, the second-generation-famous icon was already a force to reckon with in the fashion world. Notably, he designed all the clothes and accessories he wore in his debut music video.

In addition, Leo is also a model who has graced the runway of several high-end fashion lines. After generating buzz for his performance at a Moschino show, he has since walked several runways, including the NYFW.
