Street Outlaws: Is Bill Lutz Related To Jeff Lutz? Brother And Racers Family

Is Street Outlaws Bill Lutz related to Jeff Lutz? They are both parts of the show Street Outlaws. 

Lutz is an American street racer known for being a cast member of the hit Discovery channel Street Outlaws. He has been competing in heads-up racing for most of his career.

In his racing career, Lutz has wheeled and owns several cars. One of his favorite cars includes the Twin-Turbo 1967 Camaro, as it has the comfort level of a warm pair of pajamas.

Meet Street Outlaws Star Bill Lutz

Bill Lutz is also known as Big Boost on the Discovery Channel series Street Outlaws. He competes in racing events all year round and is seen catching up with fans at the events. Bill is a hero and inspiration to the new generation of street racers.

Lutz is seen in several racing events, and one might know him from the Discovery Channel series Street Outlaws: No Prep Kings.

Bill’s new favorite addition to the car collection is the latest Liberty 7-speed transmission. His other favorite car includes NEXUS R5, which he feels is easy to drive.

Lutz is active on Instagram under the username @billlutzracing. He has more than 6.2k followers and 110 posts and Bill mostly posts pictures of cars and racing events.

The street racer’s Facebook page updated the fans when and where they will be racing.

Street Outlaws Season 14 has returned on January 10. The show gives an inside look at the world behind the racing actions.

Is Bill Lutz Related To Jeff Lutz?

Bill Lutz and Jeff Lutz might not be related. Many people thought that Bill and Jeff are related, as they share the same last name.

Jeff is an American street racer who is a part of the Street Outlaws. He is a proud owner of a Mad Max. He has an older brother but has not revealed his identity.

Jeff owns several racing cars including, a CMC, 57 Chevy, 632 CI Big Block Chevy, and a 1969 Chevrolet Camaro. He owns Her Race Cars, an automobile company.

Bill Lutz Brother And Racers Family Details Revealed

Bill currently lives in Columbus, Ohio, with his family. It is unclear if the street racer has a brother, as he prefers to keep the details of his family away from the media.

Bill and his wife have a son, Kenny. His son is a crew chief and tuner, reports Haltech.

Lutz feels that the need for speed is like a family heirloom that is passed down by his father and he passed it down to his son.
