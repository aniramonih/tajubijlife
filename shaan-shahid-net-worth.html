What is Shaan Shahid's Net Worth?

Shaan Shahid is a Pakistani actor, model, writer, and director who has a net worth of $20 million. Shaan Shahid was born in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. Shahid has about 500 films to his credit and is one of the most popular faces in Lollywood. He is known for being the best established actor in Pakistan.

Shahid debuted in 1990 in the movie Bulandi. He has won 15 Nigar wards, five Lux Style Awards, four National Film Awards, a Pride of Performance Award, a Pakistan Media Award, and an ARY Film Award. He has been a brand ambassador for Unilever Pakistan and Pepsi and a Goodwill Ambassador for the One Pack = One Vaccine campaign. He has starred in many films including Majajan, Khuda Ke Liye, Soha Jora, Zill-e-Shah, Jugni, Waar, O21, and Yalghaar.
