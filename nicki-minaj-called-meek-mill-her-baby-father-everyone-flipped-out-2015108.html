Nicki Minaj Called Meek Mill Her &quot;Baby Father,&quot; Everyone Flipped Out

This is how rumors get started! Nicki Minaj sent fans into a tizzy on Saturday, Aug. 8, when she introduced boyfriend Meek Mill as her "baby father" during a concert in Burgettstown, Pa.

Video taken by a fan shows the "Truffle Butter" rapper, 32, welcoming Meek Mill on stage amid screams from the crowd. "Make some noise for my baby father!" she said, as her beau raised his arms and grinned.

Not surprisingly, fans flipped out. "OMG @NICKIMINAJ OMG #BESTCONCERTEVER guess who's pregnant," the fan who shared the video wrote via Twitter.

Minaj, for her part, seemed to enjoy — and even encourage — the pregnancy speculation. She retweeted multiple fan reactions to her "baby father" comment, replying with just a series of laugh-crying emojis.

This is not the first time the "Bang Bang" hitmaker has been the subject of pregnancy speculation. Last month, Meek, 28, told Shade 45's DJ Superstar Jay that he'd like to have kids "sooner or later" with the star. "Nicki ain't pregnant. But yeah, I wish," he said at the time.

Minaj replied via Twitter, writing, "No matter wut he, or anyone else says, Onika Tanya Maraj won't b pushing anyone1 out her vajayjay, until she's married. Now check that."

Babies are almost definitely in her future, though. In November, she told Complex that she hopes to have kids before she's put out her fifth album. (Her third, Pinkprint, was released in December.) "I feel like I was put here to be a mother," she shared.
