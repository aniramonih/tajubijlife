Harvey Grant Net Worth

Harvey Grant was born on July 4, 1965 in Georgia. He is a former NBA forward and the twin brother of Horace Grant, who became a coach after retirement. Harvey Grant had a successful career in the NBA, playing for the Washington Bullets, Portland Trail Blazers, and Philadelphia 76ers. He was a four-time NBA All-Star and won an NBA championship with the Chicago Bulls in 1996. After retiring from the NBA, Harvey Grant became a coach and currently works as an assistant coach for the Detroit Pistons.
