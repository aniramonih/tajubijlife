Pere Egbi sets the record straight about his relationship with Mercy Eke


In a heart-wrenching revelation, Big Brother Naija All Stars housemate, Pere Egbi, clarified the status of his relationship with Mercy Eke, sparking an appeal to their shippers.

Through a tweet, Pere addressed the public, declaring that he is not romantically involved with the winner of the Pepper Dem season and requested fans to refrain from sending him flowers bearing her name.

While expressing gratitude for the gestures and concern shown, Pere felt it was necessary to be forthright and succinct.

He stated, “Dear shippers, while I value your support since the beginning, I want to make one thing clear: I am not dating Mercy Eke. Please stop sending flowers with her name. This is the clearest I can be.”

Commenters pointed out the swift turn of events for the couple, attributing it to karma.

They expressed sentiments such as, “Was there even a relationship? Delusional shippers finally see reality,” and “She’s not yours, she’s everyone’s. It was just your turn, bro. I hope you enjoyed it.”

Recall that speculations about Pere and Mercy’s romance arose following their numerous public appearances and a video showing Mercy kissing Pere. However, their relationship status had been tumultuous, marked by instances of unfollowing each other on social media and misconceptions about gifts exchanged.

Their dynamics even influenced their fellow housemate, White Money, to pursue a relationship with a mystery woman. White Money’s unrequited feelings for Mercy led to a bitter aftermath, resulting in his fictitious girlfriend as a response.

During the All Stars show, Whitemoney openly expressed his affection for Mercy, hoping it would evolve into a romance. Despite his efforts, Mercy chose Pere over him, leaving White Money emotionally distraught.
