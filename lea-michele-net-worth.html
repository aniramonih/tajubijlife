FAQs about Lea Michele

Who is Lea Michele?

She is an American actress, singer, and author, best known for her role as Rachel Berry on the TV show Glee. She has also starred in Broadway productions such as Spring Awakening and Funny Girl.

What is Michele’s full name?

Her full name is Lea Michele Sarfati.

When and where was Michele born?

She was born on August 29, 1986, in The Bronx, New York City, New York, USA.

What are some of Michele’s notable works?

She gained fame for her role in Glee and has also starred in Broadway shows like Spring Awakening and Funny Girl. She has released music albums and authored books as well.

Who is Lea Michele married to?

She is married to Zandy Reich, a businessman. They got married in March 2019.

Does Michele have any children?

Yes, she has a son named Ever Leo, born in August 2020. She announced in 2024 that she is expecting her second child.

What awards has Michele won?

She has received various awards, including a Satellite Award for Best Actress in a Series, Comedy or Musical, and several nominations from the Golden Globe Awards and the Emmy Awards for her role in Glee.

What was Michele’s role in Funny Girl?

She played Fanny Brice in the Broadway revival of Funny Girl. Her performance was widely praised and marked a significant return to Broadway for her.

How did Michele start her career?

She began her career at a young age, making her Broadway debut at eight years old in Les Misérables as Young Cosette. She continued to build her career with roles in Ragtime and Fiddler on the Roof.
