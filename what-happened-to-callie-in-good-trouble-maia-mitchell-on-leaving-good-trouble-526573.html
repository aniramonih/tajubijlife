What Happened To Callie In Good Trouble? 

In the second episode of season 4 of the Good Trouble television series, which aired on Freeform on Wednesday night, Callie left the communal living arrangement in Los Angeles and flew to Washington, D.C. to apply for her dream job with the ACLU. It was also Maia Mitchell's last episode as Callie Adams Foster, the intelligent and passionate character she has played since The Fosters' debut in 2013. In January 2019, she and Cierra Ramirez, who plays Mariana Adams Foster, debuted the spinoff, Good Trouble.

Good Trouble 

An American drama television programme called Good Trouble. It is an offshoot of The Fosters on Freeform. Conceptually set a few years after the earlier series, Good Trouble premiered in January 2019 with a thirteen-episode first season. It follows Callie Adams Foster (Maia Mitchell) and Mariana Adams Foster (Cierra Ramirez) "as they embark on the next phase of their young adult lives working in Los Angeles." The third season debuted in February 2021 after the second season's June 2019 launch. The show received a fourth season renewal in September 2021, and it debuted on March 9, 2022.
