Ark: Survival Ascended How to Level Up Fast

Are you looking for the best way to level up fast in Ark Survival Ascended? Let’s go through everything you need to know.

Overall, any in-game activity will grant you some XP, so you will level up eventually by playing in the way you want. However, some activities give significantly more XP compared to others. The best way to level up fast in ARK is by farming collector notes around the island.

In this guide, I will explain the best ways to level up quickly in Ark Survival Ascended to help you progress through the early stages of the game.

If you want to experiment with these at your own pace, check out our ASA dedicated server setup guide. With your own server, you’ll be able to explore your options without worrying about other players.

Best Way to Level up Fast in Ark Survival Ascended

While any in-game activity grants you XP, some will grant significantly more compared to others. Overall, the best way to level up fast in Ark is to collect notes around the map.

Notes are small containers found around the map and grant a significant amount of XP, more than any other activity in the game. In addition, they’re fairly easy to find as they are in areas with only a few predators. The best place to farm notes is the West Zone 2.

Simply run around while gathering resources and keep your eyes open for crates. They are usually located behind large rocks or near decayed ruin structures.

Now that you’re starting to earn some solid XP, don’t forget to also check out our guide on the best stats to level up early. It’s important to spend your stat points efficiently to have a successful early-game start.

Other Ways to Level Up in ASA

Thankfully, there are more efficient leveling methods. Here are some other great options:

  • Mass Crafting: craft as many arrows or other small/easy-to-craft items as possible. The XP is worth it and the resources spent are minimal.
  • Farming Metal: Metal is the best resource to farm if you’re looking for XP. However, it is easily found only in the northern region.
  • Taming & Breeding Dinos: Tame and breed any dinos that are around your base, solely for the XP gains. Combine this with our guide on the best tames in Ark Survival Ascended to get two birds with one stone.
  • Cooking: Farm meat by killing Dodos on the beach and cook it for some easy early-game XP.

Of course, the list doesn’t end here. Any activity earns you XP and it’s important to have fun and complete activities that you enjoy, even if it means leveling up a bit slower.

Wrapping up

In conclusion, the best way to level up in Ark Survival Ascended is by collecting notes, especially around West Zone 2.

Alternatively, you can farm XP fast by crafting, farming metal, cooking, and taming/breeding dinosaurs.
