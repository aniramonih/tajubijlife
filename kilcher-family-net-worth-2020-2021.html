Kilcher Family Net Worth 2024

Yule and Ruth Kilcher, who came from Swiss and Alaskan background, are the head of the famous Kilcher Family. This family came into the limelight because of their show that airs on the Discovery channel, “Alaska: The Last Frontier.” The show documents their lifestyle of living without plumbing or modern heating and carrying out their day to day activities like farming and hunting. Their homestead is located eleven miles outside of Homer. The singer Jewel, who is the daughter of Atz Lee Kilcher, has also appeared in this show. The show which runs for 44 mins on the Discovery channel is in its 9th season.

Early life and biography

Yuke Kilcher and Ruth Weber were Swiss descendants and settled in the United States after escaping from Switzerland in the years 1936 and 1941, respectively, at the period of World War II. They were friends before, but soon fell in love and tied the knot. They were blessed with eight children, six girls, namely Stellavera Kilcher, Fay Graham, Catkin Kilcher, Wurtilla Hepp, Mossy Kilcher, and Sunrise Sjoberg and two boys, namely, Otto Kilcher and Atz Kilcher.

Yuke died in 1998, but his name and legacy were preserved even three generations later. His name is now known worldwide.

Awards & Achievements

The show won two awards throughout their career. They won the Award of Distinction at the 2013 Communicator Awards and the Bronze-Main Title award at the 2013 Telly Award. The show got nominated for the Best Show or Series at the 2013 CableFax’s Program & Top Ops Awards, the Outstanding Cinematography For Reality Programming at the 2014 Emmy Awards, and the Outstanding Unstructured Reality Program at the 2014 Emmy Awards.
