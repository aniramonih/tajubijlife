Early Life

Jakes was born on 9th June 1957 in South Charleston, located in West Virginia, and he was brought up in Vandalia, where he attended local Baptist churches. He worked in many local industries in his teenage as his father was not able to work. He started preaching in the local churches as a part-time and also joined the West Virginia State University, but can’t carry his studies and soon left the university. He continued his part-time preaching and took the job of local Union Carbide. He came in contact with Serita Jamison and later married her in 1992.

Now Jake became renowned after he became a pastor of the Greater Emanuel Temple of Faith as the church grew with time and thus gaining popularity for Jake as a Pastor and speaker. From 1982 to 1985, he ran a radio ministry named The Master’s Plan. Bishop Sherman Watkins encouraged Jakes a lot. He asked him to start a church at Charleston after ordaining him as a minister of the Higher Ground Assembly.

Jakes completed his education through correspondence courses. After completing his M.A. in 1990, Jakes completed Doctor of Ministry in Religious Studies in 1995 from Friends International Christian University. He began a television ministry too, and his ministry gained success and fame day by day.
