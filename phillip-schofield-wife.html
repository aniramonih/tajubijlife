Phillip Schofield Wife: Is Phillip Schofield Married?

Phillip Schofield Wife: Is Phillip Schofield Married?: Phillip Schofield, officially known as Phillip Bryan Schofield born on April 1, 1962, in Oldham, United Kingdom to Pat Schofield and Brian Schofield.

He’s an English television presenter who made his television debut as the initial presenter of the youth music program Shazam and gained national attention working for the BBC, first as a continuity presenter for Children’s BBC on weekdays.

He was the host of the Smash Hits Poll Winners Party, pop-magazine awards show, and also spent two years working for the Auckland-based radio station, Radio Hauraki.

Schofield presented the National Lottery Winning Lines program for BBC One between June 2001 and October 2004 and also co-hosted the BBC quiz show Test the Nation with Anne Robinson between 2002 and 2006.

In July 2006, he signed a two-year contract with ITV, a deal which also meant he could no longer present Test the Nation and was replaced by Danny Wallace. In 2010 and 2011, he hosted the annual comedy show The Comedy Annual on ITV.

That same year (2010), he launched Radio Plymouth in Devon, and in the subsequent year (2011), he was awarded the Honorary Degree of Doctor of Arts (D.Arts) by the University of Plymouth.

In April 2011 and June 2012, Schofield co-hosted ITV’s coverage of the Royal Wedding and the Queen’s Diamond Jubilee with Julie Etchingham. He also presented two series of the primetime game show You’re Back in the Room for ITV in 2015 and 2016.

Since March 2017, he has presented 5 Gold Rings, a new game show format for ITV. In 2017, Schofield presented the three-part factual series How To Spend It Well at Christmas with Phillip Schofield in which he tested the latest must-have festive gifts.

Phillip Schofield is currently the co-presenter of ITV’s This Morning and Dancing on Ice alongside Holly Willoughby.
