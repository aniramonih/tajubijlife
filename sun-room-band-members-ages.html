Sun Room Band Members Ages

Here we will look for Sun Room Band Members ages, tours, songs And merch. Sun Room Band is a surf rock band from Southern California.

Like many bands that started in the early 2020s, Sun Room started off as a quarantine project by lead singer Luke Asgian, who was growing more and more interested in music during those still times. While the people he used to play music with in high school stopped, the singer decided it was up to him to dive deeper into the art.

The rest of the bandmates fell into place one after the other, each with a similar story as Asgian.

When the world was warning them to start becoming adults, the four instead chose to follow their dreams, and with over half a million monthly listeners on Spotify, the group made the right call.

Sun Room Band Members Names

Sun Room band members names are Luke Asgian, Ashton Minnich, Max Pinamonti, and Gibby Anderson. Each member is from California.

Each member’s role in the band is as follows:

  • Luke Asgian- Lead Vocals & Guitars
  • Ashton Minnich – Lead Guitar
  • Max Pinamonti – Bass Guitar
  • Gibby Anderson – Drums

Luke Asgian – Lead Vocals & Guitars

Luke Asgian is the frontman and founder of Sun Room. Asgian is 20 years old and was born on February 14, 2003.

Born and raised in San Diego, Asgian is the genesis of Sun Room as a musical project. The entire band stemmed out of his desire to jump into music and make it a full-time career.

As such, fans and the other members of the band see him as the creative force alongside the leader of the band. The singer embodies the chill atmosphere the band is known for and reflects the same on his Instagram page where he has over 31.2K followers as of the writing of this article.

Aside from music, Asgian is very into surfing, which comes as no surprise, and loves spending time on the beach, which is plenty clear to see per his social media. As for music, Luke has stated that he is the Surf Rock lover within the group and the reason why that is their main genre.

Ashton Minnich – Lead Guitar

Ashton Minnich is the lead guitarist of the band. The Sun Room band member age is 19, and he was born on September 22, 2003.

Like Asgian, Minnich is also from San Diego and was the first member to join the group after it had materialized. According to an article by Atwood Magazine, Minnich’s interest in music, and especially the guitar began at age seven when he received the instrument for his birthday.

Minnich has been enamored with rock and roll since then and played in various bands throughout high school. As per the same article, the guitarist has been called the metalhead of the group, which puts him in sharp contrast with the rest as they mostly prefer softer types of sounds.

Still, Minnich brings nothing if not diversity to the group’s sound and is also a driving force in their music. The guitarist’s fierce playing stands in stark contrast with the rest of the group, yet helps in bringing an energy that separates the band from the rest of Californian surf music which can sometimes sound the same.

Max Pinamonti – Bass Guitar

Max Pinamonti is the bassist and part of the rhythm section. The Sun Room band member age is 20 and he was born on January 1, 2003.

Also from San Diego, Pinamonti joined the band alongside Anderson. The bassist knew Asgian from a previous band that the three were in together and jumped at the chance to join the new project.

Out of the four members, Pinamonti’s musical tastes are the most eclectic as he lives a lot of 70’s classic rock alongside some 80’s new waves. The influence has played a big part in his bass guitar work, which is spacier than the rest of the band, and as such, adds more of a surf element than if he was playing surf music as is. 

Aside from his music, Pinamonti has also earned a separate fanbase for his looks, especially his wiry mustache, which has earned him love and derision from the fanbase.

Gibby Anderson – Drums

Gibby Anderson as the drummer rounds out the band. The Sun Room band member age is 19 and he was born on November 30, 2003.

Gibby, also known as Gibson, Anderson grew up in San Diego too, however, each member was from a different town within the city. The drummer also goes by Gibby Jones and has not clarified which is his legal name.

Like the other members of the group, Gibby’s tastes are very particular, and while all of the other guys’ music tastes are rooted in rock music, Gibby has set himself apart by claiming Soul music as his main area of inspiration.

The drummer’s secret power is that, by using Soul as his main inspiration, his drumming has just the right amount of edge to take the band forward. However, given that the drummer looks entirely opposite to your average Soul drummer, Anderson has amassed his own personal fanbase that is enamored by the dichotomy he brings to the band and its image.

Sun Room Songs, Albums, And Playlists

Sun Room songs, albums, and playlists have amassed 567,188 monthly followers on Instagram. The band has seen a lot of success in two years.

Looking at their Spotify account, the band’s first release was an EP called So Del Sur, which they released on July 10, 2020, consisting of:

  • Sol Del Sur
  • Fun
  • Summer’s Here
  • Something That You’re Missing

Then, the band released a single called Just Yesterday later in the year on November 9, 2020, which would be a part of their second EP, Somewhere Tropical, released on May 18, 2021, which consisted:

  • Summer Heat
  • Somewhere Tropical
  • Just Yesterday
  • Red Dress

The band would then release a flurry of singles:

  • Crashed My Bike – July 21, 2021
  • Clementine – March 2, 2022
  • I Want You – May 18, 2022

The band’s most recent release is their third EP, Outta Their Minds which came out on February 24, 2023, and contains:

  • Cadillac
  • Kaden’s Van
  • Sunset Garage
  • Outta Their Minds
