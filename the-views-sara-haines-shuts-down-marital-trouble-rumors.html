The morning show moderator recorded herself responding to fans who filled out her "Ask Me Anything!" prompt.

SARA'S SPLIT?

This isn't the first time Sara dropped hints about a possible break-up.

Back in December, she shared a video about break-ups on social media while answering fan questions.

The host took to Instagram and posted a clip of herself dishing out advice on how to deal with the first Christmas since being broken up with.

In the video, the TV star appeared to be sitting backstage as she had her hair and makeup done.

She filmed herself in response to an "Ask Me Anything" segment on her Instagram Stories as one follower asked her: "How to deal with first Christmas after being broken up with. Don't want to answer all the q's."

Speaking very seriously as a male hairdresser fussed over her locks, Sara began: "Okay first of all, you're gonna be okay, we've all been here.

"Second, you've gotta have something ready to say, and I call this the broken record approach.

"Have a line something like, 'you know what, I'm just focusing on the holiday right now', or you can even make it humorous..."

Sara continued: "Whatever it is to make people laugh, but repeat that, the same statement, the same way each time because people eventually get it, even when they're nosey and nudging."

She finished by saying: "You do not have to talk about anything you don't wanna talk about."

Sara captioned the post: "Remember: Pick a line and stick to it! You’ve got this," and her 362,000 followers quickly commented on it.
