Travis Scott Purchases $1.6 Million Rare Maybach G-Wagon

Travis Scott is celebrating his wins. The Astroworld rapper just dropped $1.6 Million on a rare Maybach-outfitted Mercedes Benz G-Wagon. Scott took to Instagram to flaunt his new ride, showing off the custom matte deep brown varnish.
