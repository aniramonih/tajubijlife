General Hospital Comings and Goings: 2 Familiar Faces Return Jane Elliots GH Status as Tracy

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that we’ve got some coming and goings news to pass along, so let’s talk about two exciting returns and clear up a few things regarding a recent exit. For starters, we should mention that Morgan Fairchild is set to reprise her role as Haven de Havilland, the perpetually snobby Home and Heart hostess.

Now that Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) has been reinstated as the Face of Deception, she’ll be resuming even more of her duties and will apparently make another shopping channel appearance.

Haven may have a thing or two to say about that when the time comes, but her reaction to Cody Bell’s (Josh Kelly) new role may be what’s most interesting.

Cody’s set to get a new professional opportunity now that he’s impressed everyone at that Deception photoshoot.

This could lead to Cody officially becoming an additional male Face of Deception or something along those lines, so he may be poised to appear on Home and Heart alongside Sasha.

Other GH spoilers say Lily Anne Harrison will return as Andrea Gates, so Gregory Harrison’s (Gregory Chase) real-life daughter will be back for some shocking surrogacy drama.

TJ Ashford (Tajh Bellow) and Molly Lansing-Davis (Kristen Vaganos) will be taken aback by a new development in their mission to become parents, so look for a major challenge to take them both by surprise.

Next, let’s discuss Jane Elliot’s status as Tracy Quartermaine since she recently left Port Charles for Amsterdam.

Tracy revealed that she intended to go sort out Luke Spencer’s (Anthony Geary) affairs with Bobbie Spencer (Jacklyn Zeman), so many fans grew concerned about her abrupt departure.

Although Tracy will indeed spend a little time offscreen for this trip, she won’t be gone long! Jane Elliot’s run isn’t over, so look for Tracy to return to the canvas very soon.

Of course, that makes sense considering Ned Quartermaine (Wally Kurth) finally remembers he’s Ned again!

Tracy will have a chance to interact with Ned once she comes back – and that could lead to the two of them doing some ELQ plotting, too.

General Hospital spoilers say Ned will trade his guitar for a suit and a new focus on business, so stay tuned for updates on his future.
