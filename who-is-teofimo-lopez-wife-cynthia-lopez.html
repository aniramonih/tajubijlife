Who Is Tefimo Lpez Wife Cynthia Lopez?

Teófimo Andrés López Rivera is an American professional boxer.

Teofimo Lopez was born on July 30, 1997 in Brooklyn, New York to Honduras immigrants. Teofimo Lopez’s father, Teófimo López Sr., started training him at the age of 6.

Teofimo López competed in the men’s lightweight event at the 2016 Summer Olympics,  representing Honduras, where he was defeated in the tournament by eventual silver medalist Sofiane Oumiha.

Teofimo López signed with Top Rank in October 2016, and made his debut on the undercard of the Manny Pacquiao vs. Jessie Vargas fight in November of the same year.

After compiling a perfect 10–0 record, Teofimo Lopez announced himself to the world stage in December 2018 by beating veteran Mason Menard with a knockout of the year candidate.

Who Is Teófimo López’ Wife Cynthia Lopez?

On April 23, 2019, Teofimo López married Cynthia López, who is originally from Nicaragua. Cynthia was an air hostess prior to meeting Teofimo lopez. In fact, Teofimo Lopez and Cynthia Lopez first met on a Delta Airlines flight from New York City to Las Vegas shortly after López’s victorious 9th pro fight against Vitor Jones Freitas, on which Cynthia was an air hostess.

Teofimo and Cynthia Lopez got married on April 23, 2019 and spent their honeymoon in Greece.

Cynthia Lopez is almost five years older than the superstar boxer.
