Jim Carrey announces daughter Jane Carreys marriage to Alex Santana via Twitter

Jim Carrey took to twitter to announced that his daughter, Jane Carrey, married her boyfriend Alex Santana a few days ago. The 22-year-old Jane has a band “The Jane Carrey Band” and is expecting a son with Alex. Jim tweeted:

“My Jane is a married woman. Days ago her and my new son in law Alex got hitched. The day was simply perfect… lots of love here.”

Jane Carrey’s mother, Melissa Womer was Jim Carrey’s first wife, and was married to him from 1987-1995. He later briefly married Lauren Holly, and is now Jenny McCartney’s devoted partner. He’s currently gained some weight and some beard for his role in The Three Stooges.
