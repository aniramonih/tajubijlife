Is gold pyro good in tds?

The Golden Pyromancer is considered an early to mid-game tower, because of its cost efficiency and moderate damage. The Golden Pyromancer fares best against hordes of weaker enemies as it can damage them all at once.

Is Golden Pyro good?

The Golden Pyromancer, in comparison to the Pyromancer, has increased normal damage, burn damage and burn time, cheaper placement cost and the first three of its upgrades are cheaper, but it is slightly more expensive to upgrade on the last two Levels. This is considered to be an all rounder tower.

Is pyromancer good in TDS?

The Pyromancer is best combined with other towers since it deals little damage by itself but is capable of crowd control. Some enemies are immune to burn, like the Molten. This does not mean the Pyromancer cannot attack them; it only means that it cannot light them on fire.

Does pyro slow down enemies in tds?

Pyro does speed up enemies, but with the recent update, its pretty minimal, so you won’t notice it during gameplay.

What does golden do in TDS?

The Golden Skincrate is a very powerful skincrate that grants one of six Golden skins which come with a Golden Perk. It costs a colossal 50,000, the single most expensive skincrate you can buy in the game with coins. Golden Perks make towers much stronger, but usually more expensive as well…

Does Golden Pyro have hidden detection in TDS?

The Golden Pyromancer has hidden detection from Level 0, allowing it to deal with hidden enemies quite well early on. The Golden Pyromancer can also be used to drop an enemy’s defense by half when it is Level 3+.

Is Golden Minigunner worth it?

The Golden Minigunner has high DPS and a relatively cheap cost compared to other high DPS towers, like the Accelerator or Engineer. Additionally, there is no limit on the amount of Golden Minigunners that can be placed during a game, which is not the case for other high DPS towers.

Does Golden Pyro have HD?

The Golden Pyromancer is the only tower that has hidden detection at Level 0. The Accelerator, Paintballer and Outlaw (now the Ranger) used to have hidden detection at Level 0, however hidden detection was either moved to another level or removed from these towers.

Can Pyro detect hiddens?

The Pyromancer can also detect hidden enemies at Level 2+. The Pyromancer will attack any enemy, even if the enemy is on fire, as long as it can detect it, though it will prioritize enemies that are not on fire.

Is Golden Pyro better than normal Pyro?

Is military good in TDS?

The Military Base is very useful against some major bosses as they cannot be stunned. However, some enemies can deal damage to units with their attacks.

How do you get gladiator TDS?

Gladiator is a short-ranged melee tower that was unlocked by beating the SFOTH Event in The Heights. In TDS: Legacy, it is obtained by having the Gladiator badge in the original TDS or beating the SFOTH event in TDS: 2019 due to a collaboration between the two games.

Is medic good in tds?

The Medic can restore base health lost to enemies. This is only really useful early on in the game when all the enemies have low health. Later on, this becomes irrelevant when enemies gain more health, with most enemies being able to instantly drop the base health to zero.

Does pursuit have hidden detection?

Both the gun and missiles of the Pursuit can damage lead enemies and detect flying enemies. This, combined with its high DPS, makes it very useful against these enemies later on during the game. However, the Pursuit cannot directly attack hidden enemies.

Is Onyx Blade good for Pyro?

Onyx Blade it’s a good weapon for pyro Int/Faith. Good damage buffed or not.

Is the Rocketeer good in TDS?

The Rocketeer can be useful against high health hidden enemies, such as the Shadow Boss. An explosion may indirectly hit a hidden enemy on Levels 0-2 if there are other detectable enemies nearby.

Is freezer good in TDS?

The Freezer is very good at stopping fast enemies in their tracks, like the Glitch and Bug. Be aware that larger enemies, like the Giant Boss, are frozen for a shorter amount of time compared to average-sized enemies.

How much damage does the shotgun do in TDS?

Unlike other towers, the Shotgunner shoots a spray of bullets. Damage is split among 6 bullets per firerate (so the base damage is 2×6), and these bullets are spread within a small cone and can pierce through enemies.

Is Toxic Gunner a good tower?

The Toxic Gunner’s ability to slow down most enemies allows it to be an all-round tower. However, the Toxic Gunner does not gain hidden detection at any level. For suitable hidden detection, other towers must be used, such as the Minigunner.
