Know About Sid Haig; Movies, 2019, Wife, Age, Health, Net Worth

With over fifty movies and 350 TV episodes, Sid Haig took his last breath at age 80. The year 2019 became unfortunate to the actor with severe health complications and infections.

Late American actor, Sid Haig was known for his role as Captain Spaulding in the 2019 American horror film, 3 from Hell. Before commencing a proper acting career, his work was with Jack Hill’s student film at UCLA titled The Host.

Sid Haig Bio & Family

The late artist was born as Sidney Eddie Mosesian on 14 July 1939 in Fresno, California to parents Haig Mosesian and Roxy Mosesian. By profession, his father was an electrician. In one of the children’s Christmas show, when he was at age seven, he got paid as a dancer.

Sid went to Pasadena Playhouse to complete his education. The actor stood at the height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.93 m) and weighed around 74 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The 80-year-old had a net worth of $1.5 million from acting career since 1960. Some of his movies credit are 3 from Hell (2019), Death House (2017), Bone Tomahawk (2015) and Creature (2011) to name a few.

Apart from the silver screen, his major TV works include Tigtone (2018), Amazing Stories (1985) and Mary Hartman, Mary Hartman (1976–1977).

What Happened To Sid In 2019?

Sid was transferred to a medical institute after falling ill at his Los Angeles residence in early September 2019. During the recovery phase, he was infected with a lung problem that also resulted in vomiting during his sleep. Almost three years later, he took his last breath at the age of 80 on 21 September 2019 after the complication of the infection got worse.

Who Is Sid Haig’s Wife?

Apart from TV and Movies work, Sid used to cherish great matrimony of over a decade. He and his wife Susan L. Oberg after knowing each other for a while walked the wedding aisle on 2 November 2007. His wife is an actor by profession, born on 11 September 1972 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
