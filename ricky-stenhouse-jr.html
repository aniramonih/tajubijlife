Ricky Stenhouse Jr. is an American professional stock car racing driver who is best known for competing in the NASCAR Cup Series. As of January 2023, he drives the No. 47 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 for JTG Daugherty Racing. He has also represented teams like Wood Brothers Racing, Roush Fenway Racing, Bill McAnally Racing, and Big Machine Racing in the past. During the course of his career, Ricky has participated in several tournaments such as Sprint Cup Series, Food City 500, Toyota/Save Mart 350, American Sprint Car Series, National Midget Series, and has been the recipient of honors like “Nationwide Series Rookie of the Year”.
