Theodore James Andre Age: How Old Is Peter Andre And Emily MacDonaghs Son?

Peter Andre and Emily MacDonagh’s Son, Theodore James Andre, is 5 years old. He was born on November 22, 2016, in Australia.

Theo’s nationality is white and his parents are a celebrity couple.

Theo Andre got a great deal of consideration at a little age because of his dad, Peter Andre.

Peter Andre gained popularity as a singer, best known for his singles “Mysterious Girl” and “Flava”.

He is also known for appearing on the third series of I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!, and the thirteenth series of Strictly Come Dancing, in which he was partnered with Janette Manrara.

In 2009, Andre released the album Revelation, with the single “Behind Closed Doors”, which reached No. 4 on the UK Singles Chart. He followed it up with his seventh studio album Accelerate in 2010.

In July 2012, Peter Andre began a relationship with Emily MacDonagh who gave birth to Andre’s third child, a daughter, Amelia on 7 January 2014.

Andre and Emily married on 11 July 2015 near Exeter at Mamhead House. On 22 November 2016, they had a son, Theodore James.
