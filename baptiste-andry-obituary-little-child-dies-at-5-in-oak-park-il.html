Baptiste Andry obituary: Little kid dies aged 5 in Oak Park, IL

Oak Park, IL – The Oak Park community is heartbroken over the sudden and tragic passing of Baptiste H.T. Andry, who departed this world just days after celebrating his fifth birthday. Baptiste, known for his warmth, kindness, and boundless love, passed away on October 17, 2023. The community, along with his family and friends, mourns the loss of this extraordinary young boy and extends their thoughts and prayers to all those who knew and loved him.

Baptiste H.T. Andry was born on October 11, 2018, and from a young age, he exhibited exceptional kindness and affection toward everyone he met. His gentle and compassionate nature endeared him to all, as he demonstrated an innate ability to empathize with others. Baptiste had a remarkable capacity for understanding and comforting those around him, offering solace through attentive listening and sincere advice. His love knew no bounds, and he consistently supported and encouraged his friends and family, celebrating their victories and standing by them during their struggles.

Baptiste was a source of happiness and positivity in his community. Whether through warm embraces, encouraging words, or selfless acts of service, he brought joy to those he encountered. His unwavering love and kindness left a lasting impact on the hearts of everyone he touched, serving as an inspiration to all.

His caring nature made him an indispensable figure in the lives of those fortunate enough to know him, and he brought about a profound change in the hearts of people through his love and kindness.

Memorial Service Schedule:

  • 10:00 am – 11:00 am: Wake at Judson Baptist Church, 1252 N Austin Blvd, Oak Park, IL
  • 11:00 am – 1:00 pm: Service at Judson Baptist Church
  • 1:30 pm – 2:30 pm: Burial at Concordia Cemetery, 7900 Madison St, Forest Park, IL
  • 3:00 pm – 4:00 pm: Repast at Judson Baptist Church

During this difficult time, the community gathers to celebrate the life of Baptiste Andry, cherishing the precious memories of a young soul who brought so much love and light into the world.
