Getting To Know Tomeka Thiam

Tomeka Thiam is the wife of renowned singer, Akon and her nationality is African. She is a beautiful lady who is 5 feet 7 inches
(171cm) tall with a slim body figure and has a brown eye color.

She is a beauty salon owner and she works as an African hairstylist. She has not revealed her exact date of birth.

Who is Akon?

Akon is the Senegalese-American singer and rapper who is the husband of Thiam. His full name is Aliaune Damala Badara Akon Thiam and he is also a songwriter by profession. He was born in African Family on April 16, 1973, in St. Louis. 

Akon started his career by releasing his first song, Trouble in June 2004, and after that, he released other singles and gained too much popularity from his audience. He broke many records from his previous albums by winning multiple platinum certificates. Now he is married to the famous African hairstylist, Tomeka Thiam, and living a happy life with her. 

Tomeka and Akon relationship 

Tomeka and Akon have been together for about 27 years and they have been legally married for a while now. He claims to be a true African and as such, polygamy is in his nature and he has personally revealed that he has multiple wives but Tomeka is his legally married wife. 

The romantic couple has not disclosed their date of marriage. Tomeka has been the only one mostly referred to as Akon’s wife. Although her husband has multiple wives, Tomeka isn’t fazed by it. She supports him very much.

Mother of four children

The couple is blessed with four children, two sons, and two daughters whose names are Alioune Thiam, Jhavor Thiam, Alianna Thiam, and Journey Thiam. 

But there are some rumors which claim that Tomeka is only the mother of two kids not four. Since Akon has multiple wives it may not be just a rumor but it can be real truth.

Akon controversy and criminal records

He was charged for having inappropriate sexual behavior with two of his underage female fans in an ongoing stage performance in 2007 and in the same year he was also charged for throwing a fan off the stage during a concert in New York.

Controversies of Tomeka Thiam
