The Steepest Roller Coaster in the World: TMNT Shellraiser

The Steepest Roller Coaster in the World

Welcome, thrill-seekers! Today, we’re diving into the world of roller coasters, specifically the steepest one in the world.

Hold on tight as we explore the TMNT Shellraiser.

A New Winner in the Steepness Category

The TMNT Shellraiser, located in Nickelodeon Universe at the American Dream Meadowlands shopping and entertainment complex in East Rutherford, New Jersey, is a sight to behold.

Themed after the popular Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles franchise, it opened its doors to the public in 2019.

The roller coaster holds the record for the steepest drop in the world, plunging at an angle of 121.5 degrees.

Sure, here are the detailed subheadings for the TMNT Shellraiser:

Details

The roller coaster is a Euro-Fighter model manufactured by Gerstlauer, and themed to the 2012 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles television series.

It is the steepest roller coaster in the world with a vertical drop of 121.5 degrees.

Records

The TMNT Shellraiser has the same layout as Takabisha at Fuji-Q Highland in Japan, a previous record holder for world’s steepest roller coaster.

It holds the Guinness World Record for the steepest roller coaster made from steel.

Reviews

Reviews for the TMNT Shellraiser are generally positive, with riders praising its thrilling elements and unique design.

Safety

The TMNT Shellraiser adheres to strict safety standards to ensure a safe and enjoyable ride experience for all guests.

Construction

The construction of the TMNT Shellraiser began in early 2018 and it was officially opened to the public on October 25, 2019.

Design

The design of the TMNT Shellraiser includes a heartline roll and an LSM launch that propels the train from a standstill to 62.1 miles per hour (99.9 km/h) in two seconds.

Experience

The ride experience on the TMNT Shellraiser is unique and thrilling, offering riders an adrenaline-pumping adventure with seven inversions and a record-breaking steep drop.

More Than Just a Steep Drop

While the TMNT Shellraiser is known for its steep drop, it offers much more.

The roller coaster’s cars hang over the edge of its 141-foot tower for an agonizing 14 seconds before diving into the overbanked drop.

This moment of anticipation adds to the thrill of the ride.

Additionally, it features a magnetic launch that propels the cars from a standstill to 62 mph in just two seconds, providing an adrenaline rush like no other.

Other Contenders

While TMNT Shellraiser holds the title for the steepest drop, it’s not without competition.

Takabisha at Fuji-Q Highland theme park in Japan was once the record holder with a drop angle of 121 degrees.

However, TMNT Shellraiser surpassed it by a mere 0.5 degrees.

Conclusion

In conclusion, roller coasters are a testament to human ingenuity and our love for thrill and adventure.

The TMNT Shellraiser stands as a shining example of this, offering an adrenaline-pumping ride with its record-breaking steep drop.

So, if you’re a thrill-seeker looking for your next adventure, consider taking a ride on the steepest roller coaster in the world – you won’t be disappointed!
