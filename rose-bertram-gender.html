Rose Bertram Gender Is She Dating Kylian Mbappe?

At 28 years old, Belgian citizen Rose Bertram is making waves in the fashion world.

After meeting Paris-based soccer star Kylian Mbappe, she embraced the opportunity to invest in her career within the industry.

At just 13 years of age, Bertram found success after joining a modeling agency and participating in adverts for some of the world’s most notable companies such as L’Oreal and H&M.

More recently, she left everyone speechless at a Qatar fashion show when she confidently strutted down the runway sporting an utterly stunning red and white football shirt-inspired outfit – which she proudly displayed on Instagram.

Without question, it’s clear that this impressive young lady knows how to make an impact no matter where she goes or who she meets.

Kylian Mbappe’s successful career is only continuing to grow. Recently, he celebrated a momentous milestone: having his long-time girlfriend, supermodel Nora Vagner walk the runway at Qatar Fashion United.

To commemorate this eventful occasion, Vagner posted pictures of her strut on the runway as well as a video of the packed stadium.

This was not just any show; it was touted as the biggest fashion show in history! Mbappe has had a strong romantic connection with someone from Paris Saint Germain for quite some time now, indicating that this partnership may only continue to blossom.

His name continues to be prevalent in discussions about top footballers all over the world, evident best when he won the 2018 World Cup’s Golden Boot after scoring his third goal in the final match – an incredibly impressive feat!

Kylian Mbappe had an unforgettable evening in Qatar, emerging as the top scorer of the tournament but ultimately placed second to his hero and Argentina’s No. 10, Lionel Messi.

His performance over the past few weeks has reminded the world of his immense talent, yet he was left with a bittersweet finale as his team lost out on the ultimate goal; to lift the World Cup trophy.

Though awarded for individual excellence, it’s clear that Mbappe would’ve much preferred that trophy above any individual accolade it could ever bestow upon him.

After becoming only the second player to score three goals in a World Cup final since Geoff Hurst in 1966, he will leave Qatar knowing what might have been – if only by a heartbreakingly close margin.

Who is Rose Bertram?

French soccer star Kylian Mbappe has recently been linked to 28-year-old Belgian model Rose Bertram, bringing tremendous interest to her career and life.

Born in Belgium, Ms. Bertram achieved early success when she was signed to a model agency at the age of 13, and since then has appeared in a myriad of campaigns for notable fashion brands such as H&M, L’Oreal, Primark, and Agent Provocateur.

In discussing her own modeling work with Vogue Magazine, she remarked that many people initially doubted her prospects as a model.

Now with 900 thousand Instagram fans and the attention brought about by being romantically involved with one of the world’s most talked about celebrity athletes, it is clear that Rose Bertram has become an international singing sensation out from under everyone’s noses.

From a young age, Ms. Bertram was told she could not do big jobs because of her size and shape.

She has since shown that fashion is no longer about having a certain body type, it is all about showing who you are and the confidence to express yourself proudly.

Ms. Bertram, having a bold and strong character, has defied those expectations by taking part in events such as TENco in Geneva and embracing her individual style.

Her attitude reflects new thinking in the world of fashion, where talent trumps the facade of perfectionism.
