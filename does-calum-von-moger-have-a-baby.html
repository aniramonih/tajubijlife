Does Calum Von Moger have a baby?

Calum Von Moger is a famous Australian bodybuilder, actor and social media influencer born on June 9, 1990.

When Von Moger initially entered the bodybuilding world, he was dubbed “Arnold 2.0” due to his similarity to a youthful Arnold Schwarzenegger. In the 2018 film Bigger, he even played Arnold.

Additionally, he also featured in documentaries like Unbroken and Generation Iron 2 which both focused on Von Moger’s injuries and return to bodybuilding, he made appearances as himself.

Australian bodybuilder Calum Von Moger has amassed social media following of 3.3 million followers on Instagram with the account name @calumvonmoger and a sizable subscriber on his YouTube channel.

Does Calum Von Moger have a baby?

Of course, Australian bodybuilder Calum Von Moger is a father now. He has a handsome son in the name of Kyros Moger with his ex-girlfriend Nicola Segura.

As of now he is dating his longtime lover Karina Elle.
Kyros was born in 2020, and he currently lives with his parents in Los Angeles.
