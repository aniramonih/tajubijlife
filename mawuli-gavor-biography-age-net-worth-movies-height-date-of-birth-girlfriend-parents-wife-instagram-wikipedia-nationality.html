Career 

Mawuli Gavor started in the entertainment industry as a model while in the United States as an undergraduate in business management and finance. Upon returning to Ghana, he kicked off his career as an accountant. 

He, however, became a brand ambassador for a beverage company called Martini in 2013 and switched his career path. He transitioned into acting in Nollywood films and is famous for the movie Obsession. He has gone on to act in multiple other productions.

Mawuli Gavor is known for starring in movies and television series such as Eve, Devil in the Detail, Obsession, Hush, Chief Daddy, Treachery, etc.

The Ghanian has worked with top actors in his ongoing career, such as; Patience Ozokwor, Shaffy Bello, Joke Silva, Richard Mofe Damijo (RMD), Linda Ejiofor, Kate Henshaw, and Nkem Owoh.

In 2018, Mawuli Gavor produced two films and was also rewarded with the Best of Nollywood award for best kiss in a film alongside Odera Olivia Orji.

Aside from acting, he also performs as a co-host of a magazine tv show titled 53 Extra. Additionally, he has equally hosted many other TV shows in Nigeria and Ghana. As a producer and an entrepreneur, the actor has produced and co-produced many movies in Nollywood and Ghana. 

He owns a production company that has produced movies such as A Sunday Kind of Love and Entangled. He acted in and co-produced a Jamaican movie titled Joseph in 2020.

Mawuli Gavor is just getting started in the entertainment industry. With the launch of his production company, the Ghanian future in the entertainment industry is getting brighter, and more is expected.
