Brendon Small Full Overview and Wiki

The Multifaceted Maestro: Brendon Small

Brendon Small is a name synonymous with creativity and versatility in the entertainment industry.

Born on February 15, 1975, in Springfield, Illinois, Brendon embarked on a journey that would see him become a revered figure in comedy, animation, and music.

His early interest in guitar at age 14 laid the foundation for a career that would blend his love for music and comedy in unprecedented ways.

Education and Early Career

After graduating from Berklee College of Music in 1997, where he honed his musical prowess, Brendon ventured into comedy.

Among the comedians that can sing, his performances at The Comedy Studio in Harvard Square caught the eye of Loren Bouchard, leading to the creation of Home Movies.

This animated series, where Brendon lent his voice to several characters and composed music, marked the beginning of his ascent in the animation world.

Rise to Prominence

However, it was Metalocalypse, co-created with Tommy Blacha, that catapulted Brendon to stardom.

The show, centered around the fictional death metal band Dethklok, showcased Brendon’s unique ability to fuse heavy metal with animated comedy.
