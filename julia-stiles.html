Career

Stiles began her film career in 1996 with her first role in “I Love You, I Love You Not.” She appeared in the 1997 film “The Devil’s Own” and the 1998 film “Wide Awake.” In the same year, she made her film debut as the lead in “Wicked.” She eventually landed a role in a made-for-TV film titled “The 60’s” the following year. Later that year, she played Kat Stratford in “10 Things I Hate About You.”

In 2011, Stiles began filming the screen adaptation of the off-Broadway play “Between Us.” She then appeared in the dark comedy “Its Disaster” and “Girls Most Likely” the following year. In 2013, she starred in the indie supernatural thriller “Out of the Dark” and played a small but pivotal role as a reporter in the British-American film “Closed Circuit.” Julia agreed to reprise her role in “Jason Bourne,” and she also appeared in “The Great Gilly Hopkins” in 2015. In 2017, she also played Rachel in the film ‘Trouble.’ She eventually appeared as a journalist in the 2019 film “Hustlers.” She co-starred in the film with Jennifer Lopez, Lili Reinhart, Cardi B, and Keke Palmer. Her upcoming films in post-production include ‘The God Committee’ and ‘Orphan: First Kill.’ Furthermore, she is currently starring in the 2017 television drama series Riviera,’ which is still airing to this day.
