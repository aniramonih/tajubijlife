Inky Johnson Biography| Wiki

Inky Johnson is an American motivational speaker and former college football player who was born and brought up on the east side of Atlanta, Georgia, in the United States of America. Before turning to be a motivational speaker, Johnson was a football cornerback at the University of Tennessee. However, in 2006, he suffered a serious injury that left his right arm permanently paralyzed.

 Inky Johnson Story

Speaking about his childhood, Johnson says that he wanted to play football as his way to move from the rough neighborhood he was born in. There were gangs, drugs, and violence and he felt that his family needed a better life.

He trained hard doing football drills and when he joined college, he had a chance to play. His dream was to join the NFL, a dream that was close when his coach in college told him that he was a projected top 30 draft pick.

Johnson played the first game with the California Bears and won and his second was with the Airforce Falcons. While playing, he was involved in a tackle that led to an injury in his right arm.

The injury left his arm paralyzed and playing football would not be an option. He went on with his studies and enrolled in sports psychology, hoping to still be able to continue with a career in professional sports.

Inky keynote speaker

He then realized that his injury and recovery would become a great way to help and inspire others who were also struggling. That is when he became a motivational speaker. Inky speaks at schools, community centers and ceremonies throughout the United States. He was the keynote speaker at the Greater Knoxville Sports Hall of Fame induction ceremony.

Inky is also a book writer. He has an autobiographical book about his life called “Inky: An Amazing Story of Faith and Perseverance”.
