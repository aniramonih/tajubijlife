Holly Frazier Wiki Biography

Holly Frazier (nee Hatcher) was born on the 11th May 1970, in Pennsylvania USA, and is a reality TV star, best known to the world for appearing in the TV dance competition show “Dance Moms” (2011-2017), with her daughter, Nia Frazier.

Have you ever wondered how rich Holly Frazier is, as of late 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Frazier’s wealth is as high as $1 million, earned through her career in the world of entertainment, active since 2011.

Holly Frazier Net Worth $1 Million

Holly started attending dance lessons while at the University of Pennsylvania; parallel with dancing, Holly subsequently obtained a doctorate in education from the University. She later used her degree to hold jobs in educational facilities, but also used her knowledge to motivate other people to follow her. Thanks to her endeavors, Holly has received a number of prestigious awards, including EE Ford / NAIS Fellowship for Aspiring Heads of School.

However, she also continued with her dance professionally, taking lessons from Vicky Sheer, one of the most famous dancers and choreographers, and once her daughter was old enough, the two joined the Abby Lee Dance Company, and in 2011 became a part of the reality show “Dance Moms”. From that debut, Holly has featured in more than 190 episodes of the highly acclaimed show, which only increased her wealth. She became known as the Rational One on the show because of her calm demeanor, and has remained as the only original mom on the show, since the show’s first season.

She is highly respected, even by Abby herself.

Holly’s wealth also benefited from her work as an author; back in 2008 she released her first book “Moments of Clarity”, the sales of which also added to her wealth.

Regarding her personal life, Holly has been married to Evan Frazier since 1995; the couple has three children together, Evan Jr, William, and Nia Frazier.
