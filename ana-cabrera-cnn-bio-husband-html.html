Ana Cabrera Bio: Age, Parents

Ana was born on 13 May 1982 in Denver, Colorado, USA. She was born to Mexican American parents Judith Cabrera and Ronald Cabrera.

In her family, she has four siblings: three brothers John, Matthew, Nathan, and sister Angelina.

American news correspondent graduated from the Edward R. Murrow College of Communication based in the Washington State University with degrees in varied disciplines such as foreign languages, cultures, and communication.

Ana Started Career From CBS Then Joined CNN, Did Investigation

Ana, age 36, first started off her career in news from the CBS affiliates KCNC-TV and KMGH-TV as an intern where she covered pivotal events such as the 2012 Denver Presidential debate.

Later, in 2005, she moved to KHQ-TV which is an NBC affiliate as a reporter. In that time, she also worked for the FOX affiliate KAYU-TV in Washington.

It was 2013 when she joined CNN as a news correspondent and sometime later, she also became a part of the CNN Investigation team that did a report and investigated on the sexual assaults at elderly living places in the USA.

With CNN from 2017 through 2018, she has reported on some significant events as a correspondent such as covering the DACA deal and interviewing authorities and diplomats about the agreement to bring unseen insights and information to the footsteps of the viewers and audiences.
