Lisa and Lena were only six years old when they were adopted. Both sisters rose to prominence on social media, becoming the most-followed account on Tiktok. On May 7, 2019, they also deleted their page. They had over 32.7 million followers at the time. After a while, on May 7, 2020, both sisters joined Tik Tok under the same username @lisaandlena.
