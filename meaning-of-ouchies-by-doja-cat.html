Chorus of “Ouchies”

The repeated phrases “Round one, fight”, “Round two, ice”, and “Round three, bye” suggest a progression in a confrontational scenario, similar to a boxing or fighting match. By the third round, it’s over, emphasizing her dominant position in any competitive or confrontational scenario.

Verse 1

“Y’all really phone it in with the music lately”: Doja Cat is potentially calling out others in the music industry for lacking originality or effort.

“I ain’t lookin’ good? You hallucinatin'”: Emphasizing her confidence in her own appearance and challenging anyone who thinks otherwise.

“You could open up for me, who are you? First lady?”: Suggesting her dominance in the music scene, implying others are just opening acts for her.

Verse 2

“She wanna hit me I said, ‘No, Mike Tyson'”: She’s not one to be trifled with, using Mike Tyson as a symbol for a strong counterpunch.

“In 2050, they won’t know you, Otis”: Indicating that her rivals will be forgotten in the future.

“I’m the G.O.A.T, no Eilish”: Claiming her status as the Greatest Of All Time, referencing Billie Eilish but implying she’s above even popular figures.
