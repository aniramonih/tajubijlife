Conan Gray (Pop Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Conan Lee Gray is an American singer and songwriter. Raised in California and Georgetown, Texas, he began uploading vlogs, covers and original songs to YouTube as a teenager. Gray signed a record deal with Republic Records in 2018, where he released his debut EP Sunset Season. His debut studio album Kid Krow debuted at number 5 on the US Billboard 200, making it the biggest US new artist debut of 2020. Kid Krow included the commercially successful singles "Maniac" and "Heather".
