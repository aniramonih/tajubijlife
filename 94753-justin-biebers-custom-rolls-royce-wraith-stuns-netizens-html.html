Many celebrities, ranging from musicians to actors to sportsmen and -women, have a lot of money, which they made thanks to their talents.

American artist Justin Bieber happens to be among the few world-acclaimed celebrities with fortunes for years.

The star recently got people online talking after images of his futuristic custom Rolls Royce emerged online.

It is important to note that the luxury automobile designers built a masterpiece 103EX Rolls Royce Wraith which is a slick and super extreme concept car that measured 19.4 feet long.

Information gathered by We intimated that the inspiration behind the 103EX Rolls Royce was the WCC's design.

Well, Bieber acquired the ride and took it to West Coast Customs, who transformed the automobile into a never-seen-before machine.

The new Bieber's Rolls Royce Wraith was given a silver bullet-like exterior and features massive, half-concealed wheels and a super-tall grille.

TMZ reported that the star spent millions on the baby and has been turning heads with it.

We has learnt the car is a one-of-a-kind Wraith, which means it is the only car of its kind in the world.

The car sparked mixed reactions online with quite a number of people gushing over it on end. Some people, however, had different opinions and held the ride looks ugly. Many even insisted it will not be able to manoeuvrer through some cities owing to its low size.
