Smallville's Kristin Kreuk to Reunite with Alan Ritchson in 'Reacher' Series!

Kristin Kreuk is among six actors who were just added to the cast of Amazon Prime Video’s upcoming Reacher series!

The casting will serve as a Smallville reunion for Kristin and the show’s star Alan Ritchson, who recurred on the fifth season of the superhero series as Aquaman.

Marc Bendavid has been cast as Hubble, a frazzled accountant living a grander life than he can afford and gets caught up in a business he would never remotely consider. Hubble loves his family and will do whatever’s necessary to protect them,” according to Deadline.

Kristin will play Charlie. “Happily married to Hubble, the refined former debutante is living the Country Club life but is much tougher than she looks.”

Other additions to the cast include Willie C. Carpenter, Currie Graham, Harvey Guillen, and Maxwell Jenkins.
