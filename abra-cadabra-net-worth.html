FAQs about Abra Cadabra

Who is Abra Cadabra?

Abra Cadabra, born Aaron Philips on May 3, 1997, is a British rapper, singer, and songwriter from Tottenham, London. He is known for his contributions to the UK drill and afroswing genres.

When did he start his music career?

He began his music career in 2015, releasing his first track Man Down at the age of 18.

What is he known for?

He gained notable attention with his single On Deck, released in July 2020, and for his freestyle called Robbery which debuted as a single in July 2016.

Has he won any awards?

Yes, he won a MOBO award in 2016 for Robbery Remix.

What music groups is Abra Cadabra associated with?

He is a member of the UK drill group OFB.

What are some of his notable singles?

Some of his notable singles include Robbery (2016), On Deck (2020), and Can’t Be Us (with Headie One and Bandokay, 2022).

Has he released any albums or EPs?

Yes, he has released several EPs, including Feature Boy (2018), Love Or Lust? (2020), and Mixed Emotions series (2021, 2023). His mixtape Product of My Environment was released in December 2020.
