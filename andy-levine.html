Andy Levine Bio | Wiki

Andy Levine is the Founder & CEO of DuVine Cycling + Adventure Co.which he launched in January 1994. He started DuVine to share what he had realized during a post-college cycling trip in Burgundy, France, in order to really know a place, you have to know its people, its cafés, its villages, and its cuisine—the good things in life.

On that first solo bike tour, Andy learned about wine and met a cast of real, colorful characters who inspired him with their passion. Soon afterward, he started DuVine Cycling + Adventure Co. on a shoestring and a dream. The first tours were developed out of family-run Hotel Bellevue in Beaune as Andy went off the beaten track to find people for his guests to meet.
