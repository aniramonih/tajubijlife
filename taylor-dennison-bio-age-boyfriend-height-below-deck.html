Who Is Below Deck's Taylor Dennison? Her Bio, Age, Boyfriend, Height

Below Deck Down Under fans were all aware that one of the original M/Y Thalassa crew members was due to get fired. Finally, on the May 26 episode of the show, Captain Jason Chambers fired away that member and we already seem to have a replacement. Her name is Taylor Dennison and she has been working in the yachting industry for the past 5 years.

Join along as we take you through Taylor’s Below Deck Down Under stint, dating life, career, and more in this wiki article.

Taylor Dennison On Below Deck Down Under

Well before the season premiere, Below Deck Down Under teaser had revealed that at least one of the original M/Y Thalassa crew members would get fired away midway through the charter season.

Below Deck Down Under fans speculated for a long time about who would be that unlucky one, but only received clarity on the May 26 2022 episode titled 99 Problems and Thalassa Ain’t One.

Towards the end of the episode, Captain Jason Chambers fired the third stew, Magda Ziomek, and the reasons for it? Well, Magda’s work performance had worsened due to constant communication with her ex-boyfriend, Maciek.

Surprisingly, Captain Jason also fired Chef Ryan McKeown in the next episode (June 2nd) after he had failed to impress the guests with his food for several charters in a row.

Later on, in the same episode, Taylor Dennison was announced as a replacement third stew, who would be helping out Aesha Scott and Tumi Mhlongo in the vessel.

Though Taylor may be new to the vessel, she has experience of more than 5 years of working on yachts.

During her introduction to the show, she revealed that she started working on boats at 18 and mentioned working her “absolute a– off to get to where she is.” “My last boat I finished as head of service on a 115 meter crew of 19, and I’m only 23 years of age. A lot of people don’t like to listen to people that are younger. I’m like, ‘You’re an idiot,'” she added.

Taylor Dennison Career

Taylor Dennison took to Instagram to talk about her time on the show after her first episode aired. She wrote, “SO, I thought I’d try one more thing before hanging up my Stewie skort. Find me covered in hives and struggling to get my words out on #belowdeckdownunder now on @peacocktv.”

Through her words, it looks like Below Deck Down Under was her last stint as a stew. It does not seem like she is working in the yachting industry anymore.

While we don’t know how much money Taylor made as a third stew at M/Y Thalassa, the average salary for a second and third stew is around $5 thousand per month. Plus, they can make up to $50 thousand per charter season.

Prior to working at M/Y Thalassa, Taylor served super yacht M/Y Ulysses as Head of Service from November 2019 to April 2021. Taylor also worked at M/Y Flying Fox from March 2019 to November 2019 as Lead Service.

Her LinkedIn tells us that she was stew at M/Y Barbara from March 2017 to February 2019. Below Deck Down Under star’s first job was as a stew in M/Y Talisman C.

Does Taylor Dennison Have A Boyfriend?

Talking about Taylor Dennison’s love life, she has shared several photos of her boyfriend, James Taylor, on her Instagram. She first tagged her man in one of her Instagram posts on June 2, 2021.

If Taylor’s Instagram is anything to go by, her boyfriend visited her family in March 2022. Taylor’s brother-in-law uploaded pictures of them having a long lunch and captioned them: “First long lunch with fam from NZ.”

Recently, on May 30, 2022, Taylor took to Instagram to share some photos of herself and her boyfriend James from their trip to Donkey Bay Inn, Bay of Islands.

Taylor is yet to discuss her dating life and boyfriend on the Bravo TV show.

Taylor Dennison Height

Taylor Dennison Family

While we have no information about Taylor’s father, she has a sibling (sister) named Leah Vella (nee Baines). She is a makeup artist based in Sydney.

Taylor’s sister Leah is married to her husband, Johnny Vella. The two exchanged wedding vows on February 22, 2019. Taylor’s brother-in-law is a Personal Trainer at Camperdown Fitness.
