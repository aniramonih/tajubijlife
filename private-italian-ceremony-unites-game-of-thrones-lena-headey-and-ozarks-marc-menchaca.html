Private Italian Ceremony Unites Game Of Thrones Lena Headey and Ozarks Marc Menchaca



Lena Headey, famously known for portraying Queen Cersei in Game Of Thrones, has tied the knot with her partner, Marc Menchaca, amidst an intimate Italian gathering of friends and family.

The British actress said her vows to Ozark star Menchaca in Puglia, Italy on October 6, marking a joyous occasion.

Captured moments from the celebration showcased the radiant Headey, aged 49, in an elegant white wedding gown, accompanied by her 46-year-old spouse, Menchaca, who looked dashing in a smart blue three-piece suit.

Among the guests were Sophie Turner, Headey’s on-screen rival in Game Of Thrones as Sansa Stark, accompanied by her husband Joe Jonas, along with other close acquaintances.

Headey’s first marriage to musician Peter Paul Loughran took place in 2007 and ended in divorce in 2013. Together, they have a son named Wylie, who is now 11 years old.

In 2015, she welcomed a daughter named Teddy with director boyfriend Dan Cadan. However, the relationship ended in 2018, two years prior to her connection with Menchaca.

Headey and Menchaca publicly acknowledged their relationship in November 2020, having been spotted together months earlier at the premiere of HBO’s Stephen King adaptation The Outsider, where Menchaca had a significant role.

At the end of last year, a close source revealed to The Sun: ‘Lena now sees herself living in the States for the foreseeable future. It’s where the parts are for her and she’s got a blossoming relationship with Marc so she sees no point in her living in the UK.’
