American Ninja Warrior is filmed at the same studio except for one special event

“American Ninja Warrior” is an adrenaline-pumping TV show that puts participants to the ultimate test on challenging obstacle courses. While the show primarily finds its home in one location, there’s a special twist in one particular event. So, where exactly is “American Ninja Warrior” filmed, and where can you find ninja warrior gyms near you? Let’s explore.

The Main Filming Location: Universal Studios Hollywood

Most of the heart-pounding action of “American Ninja Warrior” unfolds at the backlot of Universal Studios Hollywood in Los Angeles, California. This iconic venue has witnessed the incredible feats and jaw-dropping stunts of numerous contestants. It’s where dreams are made and, for some, where they face formidable challenges.

A Special Night in Las Vegas

While Los Angeles serves as the show’s primary filming location, there’s an exception to this rule. Once a year, for the national finals, the “American Ninja Warrior” crew packs their bags and heads to Las Vegas, Nevada. Specifically, the finals are shot on a lot located on Giles Street and E. Mandalay Bay Rd. This change in scenery adds an extra layer of excitement to the competition.

Historical Locations of Filming

Over the years, “American Ninja Warrior” has ventured to various locations for filming. San Antonio and its Alamodome have been among the show’s past filming spots, offering unique backdrops and challenges. The show’s journey began on December 12, 2009, in Los Angeles, marking the start of a thrilling saga.

The Road to Mount Midoriyama

After conquering the regional rounds and semifinals, the top 15 competitors and the top three women from each semifinal earn the chance to advance to the National Finals. These finals, held at Mount Midoriyama in Las Vegas, are the ultimate proving grounds for ninja warriors seeking glory.

Ninja Warrior Gyms: Your Chance to Train

For those inspired by the incredible athletes on “American Ninja Warrior,” there are opportunities to train at ninja warrior gyms. Inside the MainPlace mall in Santa Ana, California, you can tackle five obstacle courses modeled after the show. Houston residents can join adult ninja classes at Iron Sports Ninja Warrior. In Las Vegas, the Las Vegas Circus Center offers a true ninja warrior gym experience, featuring over 310 linear feet of obstacles.

Going Down Under: Australia’s Urban Xtreme Adventure Sports Park

If you find yourself in Brisbane, Australia, and want to unleash your inner ninja, head to the Urban Xtreme Adventure Sports Park. This indoor playground offers adventure sports and obstacle courses, providing a taste of the ninja warrior experience.

Behind the Scenes of Filming

“American Ninja Warrior” involves months of meticulous preparation and filming. The qualifiers for the 2023 season were filmed from March 15, followed by the semi-finals starting on March 2Finally, the national finals took place in early May. The dedication of the camera crew and the competitive spirit of the participants result in a captivating show.

A Thrilling Journey Awaits

“American Ninja Warrior” continues to captivate audiences with each passing episode and week. Fans eagerly hope for the show’s renewal, anticipating even more incredible feats of strength and determination. As we watch these remarkable athletes conquer obstacles, we’re reminded that the human spirit knows no bounds.

My Final Opinion, “American Ninja Warrior” is not just a show; it’s a testament to the extraordinary capabilities of individuals who dare to defy gravity and push their limits. Whether on the set in Hollywood or amid the glittering lights of Las Vegas, the stage is set for these modern-day warriors to shine.
