The Alien actress Sigourney Weaver is married to Jim Simpson.

Sigourney Weaver is widely receiving popularity from her incredible performance in various films and series. Weaver is famous for portraying the character of Ellen Ripley in the Alien franchise films such as Aliens, Alien Resurrection, etc.  

The beautiful and talented entertainment personality was born as Susan Alexandra Weaver in New York City, USA, on October 8, 1949. She is the daughter of a late. English actress Elizabeth Inglis (mother) and broadcasting executive late. Sylvester Weaver (father).

Sigourney attended Sarah Lawrence College. Later, she transferred to Stanford University and majored in English. Weaver graduated with a Master's degree in Fine Arts (MFA) from the Yale University.

Married To Jim Simpson

The 71-years-old actress Sigourney is in a blissful marital relationship with Jim Simpson. Weaver's beloved husband Simpson is an American director. He has directed the films and series like Event Horizon, Space World, The Guys, among others. Jim also co-founded the Flea Theater located in Tribeca, NY.

As per the report, Sigourney and Jim first met one another in 1983 during the theatre festival. After the first encounter, the duo shared a good bond and soon start their romantic affair. 

After having a romantic affair for a year, the couple tied the wedding knot on October 1, 1984. Their marriage ceremony was held privately in front of their close ones. Since the marriage, the husband-wife duo is sharing a healthy bond as a perfect married partner. 

The director Jim and actress Sigourney visit many exotic places, attend public events, together. As of March 2021, the husband-wife duo is celebrating their 36-years (more than three decades) long marital bond merrily. 

Shared A Daughter

Following the strong marital bond, Sigourney and her spouse Jim shared an adorable girl. They welcomed their only daughter Charlotte Simpson on April 30, 1990. She is at the age of  30 as of March 2021. 

As we all know, most of the children follow their parent's footsteps. Similar to the case of Charlotte. The rising actress Charlotte is also struggling hard to achieve success in the entertainment sector.

Charlotte has made her appearance in The Guys (2002), Kelsey (2013), and Summit (2014). Besides, nothing much is known about her personal life.
