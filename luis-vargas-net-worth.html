What is Luis Vargas' Net Worth?

Luis Vargas is a Dominican musician, singer, and songwriter who has a net worth of $. Luis Vargas was born in Monte Cristi Province, Dominican Republic in May 1961. His musical styles include Latin and bachata. He plays lead guitar and became the first musician to play the bachata with the electric guitar.

Vargas self-produced his first single and also promoted and sold it himself. He formed his own independent record label called Jose Luis Records and was signed by Sony International in 1996. Vargas has been involved in a rivalry with his former guira player Antony Santos. Luis Vargas released his debut album El Debate: Merengues de Verdad in 1983 and the albums El Rey Supremo in 1988, Sin Hueso in 1989, El Tomate in 1990, La Maravilla in 1990, El Maiz in 1991, Candela in 1992, En Serio in 1993, and continued to release several albums.
