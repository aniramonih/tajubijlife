Hello there!

I'm super excited to announce that my first ever freelance project has finally been published! "Warforged! Redux" provides 6 subraces for the Warforged in DnD 5e, brought to you by Daylight Publications on DMsGuild.

They are all honestly pretty interesting and sound fun to play, as well as including references to Real Steel, Star Wars, Robocop, Bioshock, and two other original ideas that I helped bring to life. If you've ever been inspired to play a Warforged based on any of those titles, I definitely recommend to check this out! All of the inside art is done by myself. I'm really proud of it, so as an artist very new to this scene, I'd appreciate even if you just gave the free preview a look!
