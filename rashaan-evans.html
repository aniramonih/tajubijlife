Rashaan Evans | Body Measurements

Rashaan Evans is a man with a healthy build and a firm, athletically toned body.

Well, he stands 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 meters) tall and weighs around 232 lbs. (105 kg). 

Talking of his appearance, Evans boasts a tan skin color with jet black eyes and is bald. 

As an NFL player, Rashaan has to stay in peak physical condition, which is why he spends much time on his physical fitness.

Even during his off-season days, he ensures not to let a day slide without a workout. 

Additionally, Rashaan has stated that he loves working out outside independently rather than in an organized facility. 

Rashaan Evans | Early Life

Evans (fully named Alan Rashaan Evans) was born on November 8, 1995, under the star sign of Scorpio. He was born in Auburn, Alabama, to parents Chenavis Evans and Alan Evans. 

Regarding his family members, Rashaan is the second of five children, his younger brother, and three sisters.

Similarly, Rashaan’s father, Alan, was a running back at Auburn University and his mother, Chenavis, received four degrees from Auburn, including her doctorate. 

Rashaan grew up in rural Alabama and essentially had a farm lifestyle. They lived on 5 acres of open land with unfenced woods.

They raised chickens, ducks, rabbits, birds, dogs, cats, and several other animals in the facility.

The family also had several horses.

Rashaan had and still has a special affinity for horses and participated in equestrian events as a youngster.

He practiced with horses during his initial football days to improve his speed and athleticism. 

Education

Talking about his education, Rashaan Evans attended Auburn High School, located in Auburn, Alabama. 

In December 2017, he graduated from the University of Alabama with a degree in psychology.

Rashaan Evans | Career, Injury & Highlights

Career

Rashaan Evans was on his high school football team and was teammates with fellow linebacker Reuben Foster.

During his high school days, he stood as the eleventh best player in the country and also became the number one outside linebacker in the country. 

After high school, he left to play for the University of Alabama and was the second highest recruit.

Over his final two seasons, he started 14 games and appeared in three CFP National Championship contests with two victories.

The NFL Arena

After graduation, Rashaan Evans entered the 2018 NFL Draft.

Tennessee Titans drafted Rashaan 22nd overall in the first round, and he was the fifth linebacker to be drafted in 2018.

According to Rashaan, he succeeded in being the Titans’ first-round pick because of his unique hobby and lifestyle.

He signed with the Titans on a four-year deal and entered the training camp, where he competed to become the starting linebacker. 

However, he stood as the backup linebacker in his first season and later declined the fifth-year option on his contract.

In addition to that, he became a free agent and later signed a year contract with the Atlanta Falcons. 

Injuries

Throughout his professional career, Rashaan has sustained several injuries.

In August 2016, he sustained one of his first injuries when he bruised his left groin prior to the season.

Similarly, he experienced an inguinal groin tear in September 2017.

He also struggled with a thigh hamstring sprain early in training camp.

Rashaan Evans | Net Worth

As of November 2023, Rashaan Evans has a net worth of $5 million.

The linebacker primarily makes money through his NFL salaries, incentives, and endorsement deals.

On 16 May 2018, he signed an entry-level 4-year $11.5 million deal with Tennessee Titans.

However, the Titans declined a $9.735 million option for 2022.

Hence, he inked a 1 year, $1.75 million deal with the Falcons in 2022.

The contract came with all the bells and whistles, including a $500,000 guaranteed, a $365,000 signing bonus, and an average annual salary of $1,750,000.
