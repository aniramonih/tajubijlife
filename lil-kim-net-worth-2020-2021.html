Career

Lil Kim started her career in 1994 as a hip-hop artist. She has worked with record labels like Atlantic, Big Beat, and International Rock Star E1 Music. Lil Kim has worked with music stars like The Notorious, B.I.G., Junior M.A.F.I.A., Puff Daddy, Missy Elliott, Christina Aguilera, and Mary J. Blige.

Lil Kim is also an active philanthropist who has been working for causes like AIDS, Animal, Human rights, and equality, etc. In 2004, Lil Kim established a foundation ‘Lil’ Kim Cares.’ She has also been an advocate of the LGBT community; she also actively participates in several gay pride festivals.
