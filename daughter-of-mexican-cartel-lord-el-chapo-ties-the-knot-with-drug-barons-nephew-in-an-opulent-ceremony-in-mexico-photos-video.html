Daughter of Mexican cartel lord El Chapo ties the knot with 'drug baron's' nephew in an opulent cere


Alejandrina Gisselle Guzman, the daughter of Mexican drug lord Joaquin ‘El Chapo’ Guzman, has married Edgar Cazares, the nephew of Blanca Margarita Cazares, an alleged cartel money launderer, in a luxurious ceremony held in the cathedral of Sinaloa’s state capital, Culiacan.

The wedding reportedly took place on January 25, as reported by Mexican newspaper Reforma. 

The pair arrived in luxury armored vehicles while heavily-armed drug cartel hitmen provided security for the couple.

Local residents working around the cathedral revealed that they had witnessed an extravagant wedding, although they were unaware of the identities of the couple.

Sofia, who preferred not to disclose her surname, stated, ‘The church is for everyone, but the church shouldn’t give privileges to anyone.’

Another individual added, ‘With money, you can close any building here. It’s unfair.’ 

Notably, Joaquin ‘El Chapo’ Guzman, the notorious drug lord, could not attend his daughter’s wedding as he is serving a life sentence in a US prison. However, his second wife, Alejandrina, was present at the event.
