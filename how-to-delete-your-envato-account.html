How to delete your Envato account

There’s no auto delete cancel function available, however, you can request account removal using the contact form: http://support.envato.com/index.php?/Tickets/Submit

Choose “Select a Department” and complete the form with your account removal request.

More info: http://support.envato.com/index.php?/Knowledgebase/Article/View/244/50/how-do-i-close-my-account

Note that this removes your login to all Envato enabled websites such as ActiveDen, AudioJungle, ThemeForest, VideoHive, GraphicRiver, 3DOcean, CodeCanyon and PhotoDune
