Angelica Celaya and Her Husband Luis Garca Shares One Baby Boy. Know All Details Her Married Life

Angelica Celaya, whose full name is Angelica Guadalupe Celaya is an American actress and model. She is most known for playing the character of Jenni Rivera in the show Mariposa del Barrio.

Moreover, Angelica was nominated for the Premios Tu Mundo Awards for the best-supporting actress in the TV series EI Senor de Los Cielos. She has played various characters in many movies and television series. Let's know more about her personal life in addition to her professional life.

Is Angelica Celaya Married Anyone? Know Her Relationship Status

Currently, Angelica Celaya is a married woman to her boyfriend turned husband. Her life partner is none other than Luis Garcia, a Mexican boxer. The couple, however, has not shared when and where they met for the first time.

The couple dated for nearly two years and eventually tied the knot on September 8, 2018, in the presence of their close ones. Since then, the husband-wife duo has lived a healthy married life.

On the other hand, Angelica was in a love relationship with Rafael Amaya with whom she met in 2010 in the set of telenovela Alguien te Mira. And shortly after that, they started dating each other, and eventually, they engaged. 

But sadly, in May 2015, Amaya confirmed their breakup.

Does Angelica Celaya Have Kids?

Angelica Celaya and her soul partner, Luis, are proud parents of a son. The lovebirds welcomed their first baby, Angel Alessandro, on September 8, 2018. For information, Celaya already became a mother when she was marrying his husband.

As of now, the whole family lives a happy life. Like, Josh Winterhalt, they also prefer to visit new places with their son, and the couple often share photographs of their son on their own Instagram account.

Furthermore, from her IMBD, we knew that the model has the qualification to speak in three languages, i.e., English, Latin, and Spanish. She is, however, not fluent in the Mexican language.
