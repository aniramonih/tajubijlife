Getting To Know Anjali Pichai

Anjali Pichai is a famous Indian woman who was born on 11 January 1971 in Kota Rajasthan India who loves reading books and traveling. Her nationality is Indian but she holds American citizenship. 

Her cast is Brahmin and she follows the Hindu religion. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn and she is currently residing in Los Altos Hills, California, USA.

Family, Early Life and Education 

Anjali was born and raised by her parents alongside her brother, Amit Haryani in Kota which is a neighborhood in Rajasthan where she spent her childhood. Her Father's name is Olaram Haryani and Madhuri Sharma is her mother. 

Her father was an employee at the Government Polytechnic College. She did her Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology which is located in Kharagpur, India, and got her degree in 1993. Her grades in school, along with college, were pretty good ones.

Anjali Pichai is the celebrity wife of Sundar Pichai who is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. She started her career as a chemical engineer from very scratch where she worked for Accenture as a Business Analyst. 

For three years Anjali was determined in her work and after that, she moved to the USA where she started working as a Business Operations Manager at Intuit and now she is busy doing the same work. She has a net worth of $22 million according to the 2021 research.
