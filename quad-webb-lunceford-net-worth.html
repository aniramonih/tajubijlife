Quad Webb-Lunceford Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Quad Webb-Lunceford is a well-known medical sales representative and reality TV personality, famous for her appearance on the Bravo TV show “Married To Medicine”. Born on May 13, 1980, in Memphis, Tennessee, she has been actively involved in both business and public life since 2005.
It is estimated that her net worth is $500,000 as of mid-2016, which she has earned through her successful careers in television and medicine. Recently, she has also launched a pet clothing line called “Picture Perfect Pup”, further contributing to her net worth.
Despite financial challenges during her upbringing, Quad managed to fulfill her dreams and graduated from Tennessee State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology and a minor in Chemistry. She started her career with Johnson & Johnson and McNeal, ultimately earning $100,000 per year and significantly boosting her net worth.
In 2015, she joined the cast of the reality series “Married To Medicine”, which added a substantial amount to her net worth. Quad also made her acting debut in the film “My First Love” (2015) and has ventured into entrepreneurship with her own pet clothing line. Additionally, she has been married to Dr. Gregory Lunceford since 2010 and is an active philanthropist, contributing to the Canine Assistants Foundation.
