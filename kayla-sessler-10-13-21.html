Kayla Sessler's Ex Claims He's the Father of Her Son

Although Kayla Sessler has been enjoying her life as an engaged woman, her ex is starting to cause trouble.

In a clip for a new episode of Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant, Kayla’s ex, Ryan, claims he may be the father of her son, Izaiah. He demands to take a paternity test, and Kayla as to decide what to do.

Source: Instagram, @ kayla_sessler

In a teaser for the new Young and Pregnant episode, Ryan demands to take a paternity test for Izaiah.

Kayla said she was “so excited” for her fiancé, Luke Davis, to adopt her son Izaiah. “”But the other day, I got a weird text from an ex that I haven’t heard from in a long time and he’s bringing up stuff I thought we had already resolved,” she said in the voiceover.

She explained that she and Ryan dated in high school while she was on a break with Stephan, the man she suspects is Izaiah’s dad. When she first got pregnant, Ryan did wonder if the baby might be his.

“I don’t feel like Ryan’s the dad, because if I thought that I would have done that, guys, so long ago,” Kayla said in the promo. “I would have done that as soon as Izaiah was born, ’cause Stephan hasn’t been sh*t from the start.”

She added that she would have asked Ryan for a paternity test a long time ago if she believed he was the father of her son.

Kayla then said it was “a little annoying” that he waited four years before asking about Izaiah’s paternity, although she is willing to hear him out.

“It’s just been something that’s really been on my mind and kind of really bothering me,” Ryan told Kayla on the phone.

Despite still struggling with her relationship with her first baby daddy, Kayla soon found herself in a familiar situation: she was pregnant. This time, the baby daddy was a new guy named Luke, and things went differently for Kayla.

It’s true that she and Luke had their difficulties, but fans seemed relieved that Kayla found someone much more supportive. Now, Kayla’s sharing that he put a ring on it!

And it is quite the ring!
