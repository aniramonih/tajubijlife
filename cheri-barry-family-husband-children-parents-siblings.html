American politician and mayor of Meridian, Mississippi Cheri Merritt Barry was the first woman to hold that position. She passed away on Christmas Eve December 24, 2023.

Cheri Barry served the city of Meridian as its first female mayor from 2009 to 2013. She was also a longtime educator in the Meridian Public School District and the former Executive Director of the American Red Cross Key Chapter before her term as mayor.
