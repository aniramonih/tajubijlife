The Meaning Behind The Song: Witch Doctor by Devo

As a music enthusiast, I’ve always been fascinated by songs that have hidden meanings or deeper messages. One such song that has always intrigued me is “Witch Doctor” by Devo. I remember stumbling upon this song at a friend’s house and being captivated when I heard it for the first time. Little did I know that this catchy tune held more significance than meets the ear.

The lyrics of “Witch Doctor” convey a sense of joy and freedom, with the idea that monkeys have more fun than humans ever do. It starts with the lines, “You want to learn something that I can teach to you, You want to know a secret that I said is true, Monkeys, we have more fun than humans ever do.” These words paint a picture of a carefree existence, where monkeys swing from trees and eat bananas without a worry in the world.

The repetitive and infectious chorus, “Ooh ee ooh ah ah, Ting tang, Walla walla bing bang,” adds to the playful and lighthearted nature of the song. It almost feels like a chant, inviting listeners to join in the fun and let loose.

But beyond the surface level, “Witch Doctor” subtly challenges the norms and expectations of society. It highlights the misconceptions and misunderstandings that humans often have about monkeys. The lyrics state, “Humans always seem to have their facts a little twisted, They think that monkeys don’t get it in the tree.” This line suggests that humans may underestimate the intelligence and wisdom of other creatures, often judging them based on their own limited perspectives.

The song encourages us to see life from a different perspective, to adopt the carefree and adventurous mindset of a monkey. It suggests that if we could look at life in the same way monkeys see it, maybe we would find ourselves living a more joyful and fulfilling existence.

Interestingly, “Witch Doctor” is a cover of a song originally performed by Alvin & The Chipmunks, featuring David Seville. Devo’s rendition adds their unique touch of new wave and punk energy, giving the song a fresh twist while still capturing its whimsical essence.

Released in 1998 as part of the soundtrack album for “The Rugrats Movie,” “Witch Doctor” became a hit and introduced a whole new generation to the catchy melody and infectious lyrics.

In conclusion, the meaning behind “Witch Doctor” goes beyond its playful surface. It urges us to embrace a more carefree and adventurous mindset, challenging the misconceptions and limited perspectives that often hold us back. So next time you hear the song, allow yourself to be transported into the world of swinging monkeys and carefree existence – who knows, it may just inspire you to let go and have some fun!
