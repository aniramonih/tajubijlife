Anne Twist: Bio Summary

Who Is Harry Styles’ Mother?

Anne Twist, born Anne Selley is the mother of Harry Edward Styles. Anne was born on October 21, 1967, to her parents Brian Selley and Mary Smith. She has a brother called Mike Selley. In addition to Harry Styles, Anne has a daughter called Gemma Styles. The father of her two children is her ex-husband Desmond “Des” Styles, a finance worker.

Anne Twist got divorced from Des Styles when their children were still young. They got divorced in 2001. After Anne divorced Des Styles, she married business partner John Cox but they divorced years later. Anne had adopted the surname of her then-husband, John but her children, Gemma Styles and Harry Styles kept the surname of their biological father.

Anne found love again. She met her husband-to-be Robin Twist. The duo were crazy about each other. They were longtime partners before they eventually got married. Their wedding ceremony happened in the town of Congleton in Cheshire, England, in June 2013. Robin was already the father of two children, Mike Twist and Amy Twist from a previous relationship.
