Luz Gonzalez Video Goes Viral On The Social Media

Luz Gonzalez’s video has been widely circulated on various social media platforms, including Twitter, TikTok, and Telegram.

The incident was a devastating blow to the family members of Luz Gonzalez, who were left heartbroken by the tragedy

The incident took place on June 24th, 2018, when an SUV struck the little girl in a Brooklyn Laundromat. 

The video of the accident went viral on online platforms, showing the vehicle bumping up and down while rolling over Luz Gonzalez.

Her mother, Reyna Candia, who was tying her daughter’s shoes at the time, was also injured in the incident. (Watch the video here)

The driver, Jeannette Maria, was charged with failure to exercise due care by the district attorney’s office after an investigation. 

However, some believe that the charges are minor and do not hold Maria accountable enough for the tragedy. 

The video evidence of the incident should have been enough to charge her with a fatal hit-and-run as it shows the up-and-down movement of the vehicle as it rolls over the toddler on the sidewalk, according to attorney Steve Vaccaro.
