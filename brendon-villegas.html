Brendon Villegas Bio, Age, Wife, Big Brother, Amazing Race, Net Worth

Villegas is originally from Riverside, California. At the time he competed in The Amazing Race, he turned into a Ph.D. Candidate in biomedical physics at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Brendon Villegas Big Brother

Villegas’s first appearance in Big Brother was in 2010 on the 12th season featuring Rachel Reilly. He becomes faced with eviction alongside his accomplice Rachel through HOH Matt. He gained the HOH and nominated Lane and Ragan. He became nominated for Britney along Enzo and saved himself while he gained POV and Matt changed him as a nominee.

He and Ragan have been nominated through Hayden for eviction; However, he becomes evicted whilst Ragan gained the electricity of Veto. He became the 4th Big Brother Jury member this season after the on-the-spot eviction of Matt. Villegas voted for Lane to win Big Brother. He lower back to the Big Brother within the Double Trouble twist and was given Rachel, his fiancee as his accomplice.

Villegas then became immune from eviction whilst Rachel became the primary HoH and later became part of the Veterans Alliance. He and Rachel gained the Power of Veto as a team in Week 1 within the competition, Faster than a Speeding Veto, however, chose no longer to use it.

Villegas selected no longer to apply it, angering ally Daniele who had wanted him to apply it towards Jeff, causing a breakup within the Veterans Alliance. On Day 29, Brendon gained the Power of Veto for the 1/3 time in four weeks within the competition, Time’s Up, however later applied it on Rachel. On Day 34, he turned into evicted via a vote of 5-2 towards Jordan, gaining the votes of Rachel and Porsche.

However, on Day 41, Brendon Villegas defeated Lawon in a competition entitled That’s How We Roll to go back to the house. On Day 48, he changed into re-evicted via a vote of five-1. Brendon is the second one houseguest in Big Brother records to be evicted three times, the primary being Big Brother Kaysar Ridha. In the jury residence, he seemed to be very good buddies with Jeff. He becomes the fifth evicted HouseGuest and the 1st Jury member and voted for fiance, Rachel to win.
